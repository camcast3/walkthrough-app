# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II – Geofront E Guide

## Overview
For this chapter, you won't be using Rean and company. Instead, you play as characters from *Trails from Zero* and *Trails to Azure*:
- **Lloyd**: The protagonist of the Crossbell Trails games.
- **Rixia**: The girl Vice Principal Heinrich has an obsession with.

**Important Notes:**
- You only have **two party members** for the entire chapter.
- There are **no shops** available.
- Bosses are **rather hard**.
- It is **crucial** to level up your party and gather as much **sepith** and **quartz** as possible.
- Gathering quartz is the major challenge.

### Recommended Quartz to Farm
- **Defense 3** (Easiest to farm; you'll likely have many by the end)
- **Hit 2**
- **Water Quartz**
- **Impede 1-2**
- **Action 3**
- **HP 2**
- **Attack 3**

### Farming Strategy
- Exploit **Lloyd's S-Craft, Raging Sun**.
- **DO NOT SKIP THE ANIMATION** during Sepith-Up turns to farm sepith faster.
- Don't worry about CP; Lloyd has both **Lv 5 Force MQ** and a **True Gladiator Belt** equipped.

## Geofront E: Section 1

### Treasure List
| Item | Location |
| :--- | :--- |
| Celestial Balm EX | Southwest corner (after slime mini-boss) |
| EP Charge IV | Inside air duct on east path |
| Grail Locket | Monster chest (left of north switch) |
| Seven Dragon Armor | Inside west air duct (after lowering south bridge) |
| "Bakuya" (Sword for Rixia) | Northwest corner (after north air ducts) |

### Walkthrough
1. Go to the room on the **north** and examine the computer terminal to receive the **Crossbell Times**.
2. Head **west** down the hallway. You will encounter a **slime mini-boss** (small chance to drop useful quartz).
3. Run to the **middle**, then go all the way to the **south end**.
4. In the **southwest corner**, open the chest for a **Celestial Balm EX**.
5. Go back up and take the **east path**. At the end, enter the **air duct**.
6. The chest in front contains an **EP Charge IV**.
7. Go **downstairs**. There are two switches: one on the **south end** and one on the **north end**.
8. Climb down the ladder to the **left of the north switch**.
   - Clear the **monster chest** to get the **Grail Locket**.
9. Return upstairs and **flip the north switch**. This moves the ladder to the top.
10. **Flip the switch on the south end** to lower the bridge on the south end of the map.
11. Return upstairs via the **northwest ladder**, then go all the way **south** to the southeast side.
12. Climb down the ladder and take the **west air duct**.
    - Find the **Seven Dragon Armor** inside.
13. Return to the previous area and take the **north air ducts**.
14. Once out of the duct, on the **northwest corner**, find the chest with the **"Bakuya"** sword.
15. Go to the **middle**, enter another air duct to find a **switch**.
    - Press it to create a bridge in front of you.
16. Take the **west exit** to go to **Section 2**.

> **Tip:** If you need to heal, use the orbment station on the west end of the map.

## Geofront E: Section 2

### Treasure List
| Item | Location |
| :--- | :--- |
| EP Charge IV | Southeast corner (south area) |
| Strega S | Southeast path (after going west on top of air ducts) |
| Emergency Muppet | East air duct (after heading north) |
| Celestial Balm EX | Northwest (after defeating mosquito mini-boss) |
| Zeus Gem | Monster chest (after lowering bridge via southeast switch) |

### Walkthrough
1. Head to the **south area**. In the **southeast corner**, find an **EP Charge IV**.
2. Go back **north** and go **west** on top of the air ducts.
3. Take the **southeast path** to find a **Strega S**.
4. Go back to the **southwest end** and **flip the switch** to lower the center south bridge.
5. Go **downstairs** by heading **north**, then walk over the **east air duct**.
   - Find the **Emergency Muppet**.
6. Follow the **northwest path** downstairs. Keep going straight to the **southeast room**.
7. Fight the **mosquito mini-boss**.
   - Defeat it and **flip the switch** near you to shift the platform north.
8. Grab the **Celestial Balm EX** in the treasure to the **northwest**.
9. Climb up the **ladder on the south**.
10. Go past the bridge and cross the **southeast air duct**.
11. Head upstairs and **flip the switch on the south end**. This lowers the bridge.
12. Return to the previous area to find a **Zeus Gem** in a monster chest.
13. Return to the previous area and **flip the same switch**.
14. Go **upstairs** and **flip the switch on the southeast end**. This accesses the **west end**.
15. Fight a **jellyfish mini-boss** immediately upon arrival.
16. Head **downstairs**, go all the way **down** in the center to find a switch.
    - Flip it to access the **west end to Section 3**.

## Geofront E: Section 3

### Treasure List
| Item | Location |
| :--- | :--- |
| Pentagon (Weapon for Lloyd) | North end (via southeast corner and air ducts) |
| Zeram Capsule | Monster chest (west side via air ducts) |
| Seishin Robe | South end |
| Tear All Balm | South end (next to Seishin Robe) |
| Celestial Balm EX | Northwest path (north section) |
| Strega P | Southeast end (south section) |

### Walkthrough
To activate the elevator in the starting area, you must press switches at the **north** and **south** ends.

#### South Section
1. Go all the way to the **southeast corner** and climb up the ladder.
2. Travel to the **north end** over the air ducts to find the **Pentagon** weapon.
3. Return to the previous area, go **west**, and **flip the switch** to shift platforms.
4. Go **southeast** through the air ducts.
5. Go to the **west side**, climb the ladder, and cross air ducts to find a **monster chest**.
   - Clear it to get a **Zeram Capsule**.
6. Go back down, go to the **east end**, and **flip the switch** to shift platforms.
7. On the **south end**, find the **Seishin Robe** and **Tear All Balm**.
8. Activate the switch on the **bottom** to release one lock of the elevator.
9. Use the switch on the **east end** to shift platforms again.
10. Return to the starting area to explore the **north section**.

#### North Section
1. Head **downstairs** and go to the **northwest path** to find a **Celestial Balm EX**.
2. Enter the nearby **air ducts** leading to the upper area.
3. Follow the path **south** to the **southeast end** to find a **Strega P**.
4. Go **north** to find an air duct leading to the **northeast area**.
5. You will find two switches:
   - Activate the one on the **right** to release the last elevator lock.
   - Press the switch on the **north** to get down to the first floor.
6. Return to the starting area, **ride the elevator down**, heal at the orbment station, and take the **east exit** to the boss room.

## Boss Fight: Rean & Altina

### Boss Stats
| Boss | HP (Normal) | Elemental Efficacy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Black-Haired Boy (Rean)** | 237,600 | Earth 100%, Water 100%, Fire 100%, Wind 100% |
| **Black-Clad Girl (Altina)** | 235,575 | Earth 90%, Water 90%, Fire 90%, Wind 90% |

### Preparation
- **Formation**:
  - *Tanking*: Place **Lloyd** at the Top and **Rixia** on a Back row corner.
  - *Cancelling*: Both need to be on the Front row.
- **Quartz**: See "Recommended Quartz to Farm" above.
- **Accessory**: Give Rixia the **Last Resort Muppet** (she is the fastest).
- **Links**: Lloyd-Rixia.

### Strategies

#### Strategy 1: Cancelling (Focus on Crowd Control)
- **Key**: Lloyd's **Burning Heart**.
- **Execution**:
  1. Use **Burning Heart II**.
  2. Use **Raging Spin** to pull both bosses away from Rixia.
  3. When Lloyd has only one turn left for his buffs, re-cast **Burning Heart II** to prevent the "Faint" side-effect.
  4. Rixia uses **True Falling Moon** to hit both bosses. If she inflicts a status effect (especially **Freeze**), it grants more turns and breathing room.
  5. **Priority**: Kill **Rean** first as he is the most dangerous.
  6. Altina's **Black Barrier** takes 2 hits to dispel, but with this setup, she rarely re-applies it.

#### Strategy 2: Tanking (Focus on Positioning)
- **Execution**:
  1. Immediately use **Burning Heart II** and **Raging Spin** to move Rean and Altina as far away from Rixia as possible.
  2. Attack them with **Raging Spin** (alternating with **Brave Smash II**) to keep them separated and potentially **Seal Rean**.
  3. Let **Rixia** spam **Crystal Flood**.
  4. Since Rixia is faster than Rean, and Lloyd can delay him, you should be able to hit them with **Crystal Flood** and **Freeze** them.
  5. **Warning**: Keep HP as close to full as possible, especially for Lloyd. Both bosses can use S-Crafts without warning.

### Final Tips
- This fight can be very hard or relatively easy depending on your **quartz preparation**.
- Both Rean and Altina hit hard but are fragile.
- With **Zeus Gem** and **Impede 2** equipped, **AT Delay** works wonders.
- When their HP reaches **70,000 or lower**, spam your **S-Crafts** to finish them off.
