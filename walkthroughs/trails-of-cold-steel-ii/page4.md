Select your party members. By now, if you've been fishing, you should have ranked up. You can obtain a new fishing rod from Annabelle, who is at the general store. Make your preparations and exit town via Ymir Valley. Choose two members from Class VII and one guest character (Claire, Sharon, or Toval) to accompany you. Once the decision is made, you won't have the option to change your party members. This is the only segment in the game where you can use Sharon. If you don't bring her along, you'll miss this chance until you start a new playthrough.

A recommended party would include Alisa and Sharon since Sharon possesses some formidable crafts, and Alisa can serve as a CP battery, making them a very strong pair.

This boss has a ton of HP, it is also crazy strong. The boss will use a skill called drumming which will heal itself and greatly buff it's attack, defense, and speed. Allowing it to strike you multiple times, this means the boss can wipe your party fast. Not only does it hit hard, but it can also cause faint on your party with each attack. The most effective way of killing this boss is to rely on flame type arts. It does the most amount of damage without using Crafts. You can also use Overdrive for an instant magic cast. Once you take out about half its HP, you can have your party spam their S-Craft before the boss has a chance to heal itself from its buff.

This is an unwinnable boss fight, but to fulfill the bonus condition for the extra 3 AP, you must reduce the boss's HP to below 50% within 40 turns. The challenge lies in the boss's exceptionally high defense, which renders regular attacks almost ineffective. The most effective strategy is to enhance your party with Jusis's buffs and unleash a barrage of S-Crafts. If your CP is depleted, resort to using arts. Beware, if the boss manages to cast his arts, it can deal a devastating 17,000 damage to each party member, which will K.O. them in an instant. Therefore, it's crucial to have a character capable of canceling his art casting to prevent this issue. After inflicting a substantial amount of damage, the boss will employ his S-Craft to instantly decimate your entire party.
Following this encounter, you'll engage in another mech battle, but it's also an unwinnable one. After this fight, the chapter will conclude.
