## December 9

### Party Selection
Select your party members. By now, if you've been fishing, you should have ranked up. You can obtain a new fishing rod from **Annabelle**, who is at the general store. Make your preparations and exit town via **Ymir Valley**.

Choose **two members from Class VII** and **one guest character** (Claire, Sharon, or Toval) to accompany you. Once the decision is made, you won't have the option to change your party members.
*   **Important:** This is the only segment in the game where you can use **Sharon**. If you don't bring her along, you'll miss this chance until you start a new playthrough.
*   **Recommended Party:** **Alisa** and **Sharon**. Sharon possesses formidable crafts, and Alisa can serve as a CP battery, making them a very strong pair.

### Ebel Highway
You will end up at **Ebel Highway 2**. Pick up the **Flame Tongue** quartz near Valimar. Then head directly to **Legram** by going south. The game won't let you fully explore Ebel Highway yet, so pick up the rest of the treasure chests later.

### Legram
Watch the event scene at the **Bracer Guild**. Then go to **Aprikoze Inn** and speak to **Weiber** behind the counter; he will give you a copy of *Gambler Jack II - Chapter 6*. Take the back door outside and speak to **Old Man Bem**; he will teach you the **Seafood Pilaf** recipe.

Head to the **General Store** and purchase *Gambler Jack Reprint #4*, and stock up on new equipment.
*   **Thors Academy Students:** **Vivi** is at the Church, and **Casper** is at the Training School.
*   **Fishing:** If you go to the harbor, you can find a fishing spot near **Klaus**.

When you are ready, speak to **Klaus** to go to **Lohengrin Castle**. Climb up the hill and go inside; you will fight a boss shortly after.

#### Boss: Zelvenom
*   **HP:** 45,225
*   **Elemental Efficacy:** Earth (110), Water (100), Fire (90), Wind (100), Time (100), Space (100), Mirage (140)
*   **Unbalance Efficacy:** Slash (★★★☆), Thrust (★☆☆☆), Pierce (★★☆☆), Strike (★☆☆☆)
*   **Boss Skills:**
    *   **Poison Breath:** Area (L). Inflicts Poison.
    *   **Tail Swipe:** Area (L). Push back.

**Strategy:** This boss is quite easy compared to previous fights. It has a strong area attack that can cause poison, but you should be able to defeat it before it becomes a hassle. Since the boss has very poor slash resistance, deal damage with Slash-type characters like **Laura** and **Rean**. It is also weak against Mirage arts (Emma). Status ailments work well. If **Sharon** is in your party, the fight is super easy due to her area healing craft that removes status ailments.

After the event scene, **Laura** and **Emma** will join your party. Head to the **Bracer Guild** (southeast) and speak to **Miles** for side quests.

### Side Quest: Arseid School Bout 2
Go to the **Arseid School Training Hall** and speak to **Klaus** or **Gaveli** at the arena. Members of the Arseid School want a rematch. Select the first choice to fight them in a duel. Rean and Laura are automatically included; select two other members.
*   *Note: You cannot use guest characters for this fight.*

**Bosses:**
*   **Fritz** (36,352 HP)
*   **Ares** (27,440 HP)
*   **Gaveli** (36,707 HP)
*   **Datt** (27,440 HP)

**Strategy:** Students are weak against status ailments. **Laura** has a good chance of causing Faint; **Rean's Gale** can Seal them. They are also easily blinded. If their HP gets low, they buff and heal, becoming dangerous with area skills dealing 5000+ damage. You can cheese this with S-Crafts, or drag it out for analysis.
*   **Reward:** Heal quartz, 1,500 Mira, 4 AP.

### Side Quest: Ebel Rebel
Head out through **Ebel Highway**. The quest monster is at **Ebel Highway 2** near the entrance to Ebel Highway 3.
*   *Recommendation:* Equip a Faint-resistant accessory.

**Boss: Gordi Schnarr G**
*   **HP:** 98,467
*   **Elemental Efficacy:** Earth (120), Water (100), Fire (140), Wind (100), Time (100), Space (100), Mirage (100)
*   **Unbalance Efficacy:** Slash (★★☆☆), Thrust (★☆☆☆), Pierce (★☆☆☆), Strike (★☆☆☆)
*   **Boss Skills:**
    *   **Slam:** Area (M). Inflicts Faint.
    *   **Roar:** Heal + Str/Spd 50%.
    *   **Charge Slam:** Takes 2 turns. Inflicts Faint (Can be Cancelled).

**Strategy:** The boss has massive HP and is very strong. It uses "Drumming" to heal and buff Str/Def/Spd, allowing multiple hits. It can cause Faint and deals nearly 7000 damage with "Hard Hit" when its buff expires.
*   **Best Approach:** Use **Flame** type arts for maximum damage. Use Overdrive for instant magic casts. When the boss is at ~50% HP, spam S-Crafts before it can re-buff.
*   **Reward:** Sepith Mass x300, 1,500 Mira, 4 AP.

After defeating the boss, you can instantly return to Legram. Report to **Miles** at the Bracer Guild to finish the quest.

### Ebel Highway 3 & Aqua Shrine
Return to **Ebel Highway**. Take the north exit to **Ebel Highway 2**, examine **Valimar** for a short event, then take the northeast exit to **Ebel Highway 3**.

Head to the **northwest exit** to reach **Aqua Shrine**. Clearing this gives **3 extra AP**. There is also a fishing spot near the waterfall.

#### Aqua Shrine Exploration
1.  **Entry:** Head downstairs, open the gate.
2.  **Water Level 1:** Follow the west path to the **northwest corner**. Press the switch to lower the water level.
3.  **Trial Chest:** Follow the west path, then go all the way east. Defeat the Trial Chest Boss for a reward.
4.  **Monster Chest:** Follow the south path. When you see stairs, take the **north path** to find a monster chest containing the **Scent** quartz.
5.  **Treasure:** Backtrack to the south stairs. Find **Blue Scaled Shoes** in a treasure box.
6.  **Water Level 2:** Go to the west side. Open the chest for an **EP Charge II**, then push the switch to raise the water.
7.  **Boss Path:** Take the center path to the northwest area.

**Trial Chest Boss: King Crysnetic**
*   **HP:** 33,157
*   **Drop:** Shining R
*   **Strategy:** Easy fight. Enemies cause Mute with area attacks. Use **Laura's S-Craft** or **Brilliant Spin** to clear mobs. Focus on the boss with Emma's Arts and Laura's attacks.

**Boss: Tildawn Glacia x3**
*   **HP:** 43,097
*   **Elemental Efficacy:** Earth (100), Water (0), Fire (120), Wind (100), Time (130), Space (130), Mirage (130)
*   **Strategy:** Similar to previous Tildawns but causes Mute. If your party relies on magic, cancel their casting with **Impede** crafts (Rean, Machias, Alisa, Fie). To cheese: Rean in Overdrive, Motivate x2, then S-Craft spam.
*   **Reward:** Curia Balm, U-Material.

After the boss, take the portal to exit. Collect any remaining chests in Ebel Highway 3, then take the **northeast exit** to **South Kreuzen Highway**.

### South Kreuzen Highway
*   **Bridge Scene:** Move forward for a short scene.
*   **Northwest Corner:** Fishing spot and **Hydro Cannon** quartz.
*   **Northeast Corner:** **Sepith Mass** in a chest.
*   **North Region (Split Path):**
    *   West: **Tearal Balm**.
    *   East: Monster chest with **Tauros Master Quartz**.
*   **Exit:** Return to center, take north exit to **Bareahard**.

*Warning: Entering Bareahard advances the story. Finish all Legram side quests first.*

### Hidden Quest: St. Veronica's Tears
1.  Go to **Bareahard Cathedral** (Central Plaza, north end). Speak to **Sister Tatiana** (west room).

*   **HP:** 88,111

### Conclusion of Bareahard

2.  Visit **Sorciere Restaurant** and speak to **Hammond**.
3.  Head to **Station Street**, take south exit to **Bareahard Airport**.
    *   *Warning:* This advances the story. Ensure all side activities are done.

### Bareahard Airport & Airship
1.  **Hold (Downstairs):** Find **Freeze** quartz.
2.  **3F (Lounge):** Play the racing mini-game.
    *   **Boss: Jusis** (19,500 HP). Beating him gives **2 AP**. Retry if you lose.
3.  **Boss Battle:** Face **Duvalie** and **McBurn**.
    *   **Duvalie:** 84,208 HP. **McBurn:** 256,800 HP.
    *   **Bonus Condition:** Defeat Duvalie before McBurn destroys you to get **3 Extra AP**.
    *   **Strategy:** Buff strength, spam S-Crafts on Duvalie. Use Zeram Powder to revive for 100 CP if needed. Scan enemies.
    *   **Outcome:** Alternate scene if Duvalie falls. Followed by an unwinnable mech battle.

**Mech Battle: Hector & Armored Vehicle NP-II**
*   **Strategy:** Defeat the **Armored Vehicle** first (lowest HP). It has 3-star unbalance weakness in attack stance. Then focus on **Hector** (Attack head in idle stance, body in attack stance).
*   **Reward:** Save point, followed by a hot spring scene. Choose a partner for **600 Link Exp**.

### Final Boss: Rufus
**Boss:** Rufus
*   **HP:** 128,640
*   **Item Drop:** None
*   **Elemental Efficacy:** Earth (90), Water (90), Fire (90), Wind (90)
*   **Unbalance Efficacy:** Slash (☆☆☆☆), Thrust (☆☆☆☆), Pierce (☆☆☆☆), Strike (★☆☆☆)

**Strategy:**
This is an **unwinnable** boss fight. However, to fulfill the bonus condition for the extra **3 AP**, you must reduce the boss's HP to **below 50% within 40 turns**.
*   **Challenge:** The boss has exceptionally high defense, rendering regular attacks almost ineffective.
*   **Best Approach:** Enhance your party with **Jusis's buffs** and unleash a barrage of **S-Crafts**. If your CP is depleted, resort to using Arts.
*   **Warning:** If Rufus manages to cast his Arts, it can deal a devastating **17,000 damage** to each party member, instantly K.O.ing them. It is crucial to have a character capable of **canceling his Art casting** to prevent this.
*   **Outcome:** After inflicting substantial damage, the boss will employ his S-Craft to instantly decimate your entire party.

Following this encounter, you'll engage in another **mech battle**, but it is also unwinnable. After this fight, the chapter will conclude.
