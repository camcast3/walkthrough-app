# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
## Act 2 Part 1

> **Critical Opening Tip:** Watch the opening sequence; the guest character with the highest Link EXP (Must be level 3) will give you a powerful accessory.

| Character | Accessory | Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Claire | Cerulean Hearts | ACC +100%, Critical Rate +10% |
| Sharon | Black Virtue | Attacks/Craft can cancel arts (90% chance). Delay +6 |
| Toval | Quick Calibur S | Reduce Arts cast time by 1/3 |

> **Party Assembly:** Read the quest list at the computer terminal. **Gaius** is a mandatory member.
> **Fresh Nightmare Mode Tip:** If you need to use the Tri-colored Onigiri trick, use Emma.
> **Resource Management:** From now on, spend your mira and sepith only on the four characters you intend to take with you to face the Act boss. Do not invest in support characters unless you plan to use them. Swap equipment and quartz among support characters to ensure they gain experience. This minimizes expense while keeping gear updated. When the story requires specific characters, simply transfer their armor, boots, and quartz. Save as much mira and sepith as possible until further notice.

## December 15: The Courageous

Explore the airship. The **Courageous** will be your new base of operation. Visit locations marked with a **red exclamation mark**.

*   **1st Floor:** Speak to George and Valimar. After this event, Rean can use his Spirit Unification at the cost of 100 CP.
*   **2nd Floor (Cafeteria):** Speak to Millium for a short event. Play the **Blade Mini Game** in the leisure room (regardless of win/loss, playing raises Link EXP by 100 for the first 5 rounds).
*   **Royal Room:** Visit Princess Alfin.

### Courageous's Outline
*Note: Not all facilities are available yet. You need to recruit Thors Academy Students to unlock functions.*

*   **5th Floor - Bridge:**
    *   **Computer Terminal:** Hub for quests, reporting, and student intel.
    *   **Captain Towa:** Rearrange party, move destinations, or progress the main storyline.
*   **4th Floor - Assembly Area:**
    *   **Royal Room:** Speak to Alfin to receive parts of the **Phoenix Chapter** (medals based on achievements).
    *   **Training Facility:** Fight enemies under conditions for U-Materials and Master Quartz.
*   **3rd Floor - Shop Area:**
    *   **Weapon/Item Shop:** NPC Casper.
    *   **Accessory Shop:** NPC Colette.
    *   **Premium Products:** NPC Hugo.
    *   **Radio Sweepstakes:** NPC Munk (Lottery system).
    *   **Recipe Gift:** Offer recipes to Bridget for rewards.
*   **2nd Floor - Cafeteria/Multipurpose Area:**
    *   Club area (Fishing, Literature, etc.).
    *   **Blade Mini Game:** Increases Link EXP (first 5 rounds only).
*   **1st Floor - Engineering Area:**
    *   **Engineering:** Synthesize new quartz with George.
    *   **Hangar:** Customize Orbal Bike. (Later: Clara unlocks EX-Orb function for Valimar).

After exploring, speak to **Towa** to recruit missing students. Destinations: **Ymir, Proving Ground, Garrelia Fortress, Nord Highlands, Legram**. This guide starts with **Garrelia Camp**.

## Searching for Friends (Garrelia Section)

### Proving Ground
Select **Proving Ground** (middle right of world map). Bring **Laura** to your party.
*   **Store Area:** Speak to **Monica** to recruit her.
    *   *Role:* Found on 3rd Floor. If you give her Supreme Dishes, she rewards items. Cook every Supreme Dish for a rare quartz.
*   **Center of Camp:** Speak to **Rex** to add his profile to the Notebook.
*   **Officer Virginie:** Lady by the truck. She teaches the **Tomato Burger Recipe**.
*   **Headquarters (East Exit):** Speak to **General Craig** to start the side quest.

### Side Quest: Without Leave
1.  Head to **Garrelia Camp Headquarters**. Speak to General Craig and accept the request.
2.  Gather info from NPCs marked with **green exclamation marks**:
    *   Lunch table (Southeast corner).
    *   Clerks (Resting area).
    *   Clerks (Store area).
3.  Speak to the soldier near the **west gate exit**.
4.  Head to **Garrelia Fortress - Ruins**.
5.  Speak to Soldiers near the tank (west side, near Garrelia Byroad exit).
6.  Head to the east end near the **crater**. An event occurs, ending the quest.
    *   *Note:* No monsters here. Use the **Orbal Bike** (Press Square).
    *   **Treasure Chests:**
        *   Adamant Shield (Garrelia Fortress)
        *   Sepith Mass x400
        *   Crystal Flood
    *   **Trial Chest (Southwest Corner):** Defeat the boss to unlock **Overdrive** for **Fie** and **Sara**.
        *   *Boss:* Huge Rat & Dirty Rats.
        *   *Strategy:* Use full CP and S-Crafts, or Sara's Arts (boss has poor magic defense).

**Post-Quest Event:** A giant monster (**Cryptid**) appears near the crater.
*   *Warning:* Recommended for Level 100. Possible at Level 75+ with correct setup.
*   *Reward:* Special quartz for **Lost Arts**.
*   *Strategy:* Best to hunt on N3 run when Jusis is Lv 81 (Platinum Shield) or wait until all 5 are available (Ch 2-3).

### Garrelia Byroad
1.  Exit to **Garrelia Byroad 2** (East).
2.  Travel to **Garrelia Byroad 1**.
3.  Go to the **northeast corner** (Trial Chest location) to find **Emily** and **Nicolas**.
4.  Recruit them and help install the **relay machine** (6 points total: 3 on Byroad 1, 3 on Byroad 2).
5.  Report to Emily and Nicolas, then return to Courageous.

## Searching for Friends (Legram Section)

### Preparation
Return to Courageous, speak to **Towa**, and set destination to **Legram** (bottom of map).
*   **General Store:** Buy **Imperial Chronicle Issue #4** and **Gambler Jack Reprint #8**.

### Recruitment
*   **Chapel (Right Room):** Speak to **Vivi** (Need **Fie** in party to convince her).
*   **Training Hall (Left Room):** Speak to **Casper** (Need **Imperial Chronicle Issue #4**).
*   **Bracer Guild:** Event with Miles.
*   **Aprikoze Inn:** Start side quest.

### Side Quest: Badass in Danger!!!
1.  Speak to the **green-haired boy** at Aprikoze Inn.
2.  Find **Patiry** in **Ymir** (speak to Luke, the station guard by the pub).
3.  Return to Courageous, travel to **Ymir**.
4.  Speak to Patiry near the station guard to finish.
    *   *Reward:* Bunny Watch, 3000 Mira, 4 AP.

### Ymir
Return to Courageous, travel to **Ymir** (Northern section).
*   **General Store:** Buy **Gambler Jack Reprint #9** and **Crunchy Rice Cracker** recipe.
*   **Activities:** New Snow Board course, fish in Ymir Valley.

## Searching for Friends (Nord Section)

### Zender Gate & Watchtower
1.  Speak to Towa, destination **Zender Gate** (Top right).
2.  Enter Zender Gate, head north to **Tank Officer Hoover**. Get **Gambler Jack II - Chapter 8**.
3.  Leave Zender Gate. Find **Beryl** at the stone monument (Northwest corner).
    *   *Note:* If missed, she appears in Legram (Dec 19) or Terra Shrine (Dec 23).
4.  Use Quick Navigation to **Watchtower**.
5.  Speak to **Mint** (first choice to help). Examine key points (red exclamation marks) on every floor.
6.  Head to the **rooftop** for an event. Mint joins Courageous.

### Story Quest: Find Our First Years
Recruit the following students to finish:
*   Monica (Garrelia Proving Ground)
*   Vivi (Legram Chapel)
*   Casper (Legram Training Hall)
*   Mint (Watchtower)

**Completion:** Return to Courageous, Bridge (5F), Computer Terminal, select 2nd option.
*   *Reward:* Sepith Mass x400, 2500 Mira, 4 AP.

### Lake Lacrima & Monster Madness
1.  Speak to Towa, destination **Lake Lacrima**.
2.  **General Store:** Buy **Gambler Jack Reprint #7**.
3.  Speak to **Sheeda** for **Hearty Milk Hotpot** recipe.

**Story Quest: Monster Madness**
1.  **Cabin:** Speak to Elder Ivan or Gwen.
2.  **Nord Highlands - North:** Find quest monster near fishing spot (Northeast).
    *   *Boss:* Magic Knight Ortheim (91808 HP).
    *   *Strategy:* Full party. Spam S-Crafts when HP is low to prevent healing.
3.  **Reward:** Sepith Mass x400, 2500 AP (Note: Text says AP, likely meant Mira or AP), 4 AP.

### Ancient Quarry
1.  Go to **Nord Highlands - North**, use Quick Nav to **Ancient Quarry**.
2.  **Entrance:** EP Charge II.
3.  Speak to **Clara** (First choice) to explore.
4.  **Center (Path Split):** Trial Chest for **Emma, Fie, Gaius, Jusis**.
    *   *Boss:* Alpha Diglem x2 & Diglem x4.
    *   *Reward:* Unlock Overdrive for these characters.
5.  **South End:** Outside area. **Volcanic Rain** Quartz.
6.  **North Path:** **Pandora** Master Quartz (Monster Chest).
7.  **East:** U-Materials.
8.  **North End:** Boss Fight.
    *   *Boss:* **Ginosha-Zanak** (Giant Spider).
    *   *Note:* Returning boss from CS, weakened. Summons minions (max 4).
    *   *Strategy:* Status ailments (Confused, Sleep, Faint) work well.
    *   *Reward:* Clara joins Courageous.
    *   *New Features:* EX-Orb function for Valimar, **Awakener's Call** ability.
    *   *New Game+ Only:* Return to boss spot to find **Black Records #1**.

**End of Day:**
1.  Report status at Computer Terminal (Bridge).
2.  Speak to Towa, select **"Commence Operation"**.

> **Nightmare Tip:** If you got Sharon's **Black Virtue**, equip it on Rean permanently. Combined with Impede 1-2, it makes Arc Slash incredibly effective.

## December 17: Twin Dragons Bridge

**Party Setup:** **Eliot** and **Sara** are mandatory. No turning back after selection.

### Mech Battle
Face three mechs. Focus on defeating one at a time.
*   **Spiegel (Spear):** Backlines (buffs). Force to front by killing others. Weakness: Body.
*   **Hector:** Weakness: Head (Idle), Body (Attack).
*   **Drakkhen:** Weakness: Arms (Both stances).
*   *Strategy:* Save Brave points/CP for the second unit. Heal if HP < 35k-40k.

### Twin Dragons Bridge Exploration
*   **2F:**
    *   Right Room: **EP Charge III**.
    *   Center -> West Duct: **Flood** Quartz (Monster Chest).
    *   Center -> East Path -> Southwest Duct: **Curia Balm**.
    *   Center -> East Path -> East End: **Ebony Boots**.
    *   Southeast Corner: Upstairs.
*   **3F:**
    *   West Side (via duct): Second Room -> **Tearal Balm**.
    *   Southeast Corner (break boxes): **Yggdrasil** Quartz.
    *   Center -> Left Treasure: **Ebony Vest**.
    *   Right Duct: Downstairs.
    *   East Duct: Back to 3F.
    *   North Stairs: Up to 4F.
*   **4F:**
    *   North Room: **Sepith x200**.
    *   Center: Heal, then fight boss.

**Boss:** Kaiser Doven B & Kaiser Doven R.
*   *Strategy:* Easy. Use Eliot's **Resounding Beat** to heal. Wipe out summoned mobs immediately.
