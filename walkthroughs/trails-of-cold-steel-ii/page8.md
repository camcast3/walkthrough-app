# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
## Act 2 Part 3

### December 23

When you regain control, speak to **Towa** twice on the Bridge. A list of new locations will be added, such as **Eisengard Range** and **Roer Region**.

#### Initial Actions
*   **4th Floor (Training Facility):** Speak to **Emily** and **Alan** to update their character profiles. You can also speak to **Alfin** for a short event.
*   **2nd Floor:** Speak to **Dorothee** and purchase the **"Pasta"** recipe.
*   **1st Floor:** Speak to **Angelica** to update her profile.
*   **Blade:** Play some Blade with friends to build Link EXP.

> **Note:** The final two **Cryptids** will appear: one in **Nortia Highway 2** and one in **Eisengard Mountain 2**.

#### Side Quest: The Sweetest of Challenges
Head to the **Infirmary** (2nd Floor) and examine the card on the desk to start the quest.
1.  Class VII gathers in the meeting room. Listen to everyone's opinions and repeat them.
2.  **Select the Culprit:** Choose **Machias** (2nd choice) to earn **2 extra AP**.
    *   *Note: Picking Emma gives only 1 AP on the latest Japanese patch.*
3.  **Rewards:** Thief Cloak, 3500 Mira, 3 (+1-2) AP.

#### Recruiting Remaining Students
1.  **Roer:**
    *   Obtain any missing Trial Chests from the previous act.
    *   Go to **RF Store**, speak to **Hugo**, and accept his request.
    *   Leave Roer, go to **Legram**, and speak to **Watteau** at Watteau's Store.
    *   Go to **Ymir's General Store** and speak to **Lio**.
    *   Visit the **snowboard area** (new course added).
    *   Return to **Roer** and speak to **Hugo** again. He joins the *Courageous* and opens the premium store.
2.  **Roer Institute of Technology (Northwest Building 2F):**
    *   Speak to **Ratto** (top corner) and accept his request.
    *   Leave town via **Nortia Highway 2**.
    *   Head to the **southwest corner** to find **Stefan**.
    *   **Requirement:** Bring **Machias** in your party to trigger an event battle.
    *   **Reward:** Stefan joins the *Courageous*.
    *   *Optional:* Fight the Cryptid **Agnagarn** on the southeast end of Nortia Highway 2.
3.  **Nortia Highway 3:**
    *   Go to the fishing spot (northwest of the map) and speak to **Kenneth**.
    *   Accept his request and catch a **Glutinous Bass** at the spot beside him.
    *   Speak to Kenneth again; he joins the *Courageous*.
4.  **Schwarz Drache Barrier (Southwest exit of Nortia Highway 3):**
    *   Speak to **Rex** near the gate to update his bio and receive *Gambler Jack II - Chapter 11*.
    *   Enter the resting area and speak to **Margarita** (with **Millium** in party). She joins the *Courageous*.
        *   *Tip:* Giving Margarita every **Peculiar Dish** rewards rare items and a rare quartz.
    *   Speak to the lady by the counter to learn the **"Tomato Stew"** recipe.
    *   Return to **Courageous** (Cafeteria) and speak to **Nicholas** to update his profile.
5.  **Eisengard Range:**
    *   Return to the Bridge, speak to Towa, and set destination to **Eisengard Range**.

#### Side Quest: Gone Air
1.  Watch the event scene (automatic fight with two Jaegers).
2.  You arrive in the airship from the end of Chapter 1.
    *   **Northwest corner:** Pick up **Freeze Quartz** (if missed previously).
3.  **Lobby Room:** Fight a group of Jaegers.
4.  **Northwest Room:** Defeat a single Jaeger to get **Card Key 1**.
5.  **3rd Floor (Southeast entrance):** Defeat four Jaegers to get **Card Key 2**.
6.  **4th Floor (South entrance):** Defeat five Jaegers to finish the quest.
    *   **Rewards:** **Klein** and **Hibelle** join the *Courageous*.
    *   *2nd Playthrough+:* Speak to **Norton** at **Roer's Airport** to receive *Black Records #4*.
7.  Return to *Courageous* and speak to **Klein** on the Bridge to update his profile.

#### Eisengard Mountains & Trial Chests
*   Revisit **Eisengard Mountains** landing spot for a **Trial Chest** (Fie & Laura).
    *   Clearing it unlocks their **Overdrive Function**.
    *   *Boss:* **Rolling Rock** (HP: 42,809). Weak to Faint.
*   New enemies appear: Shadow Dancer, Venom Demon, Steel Golem.
*   **Final Cryptid:** **Lindbaum** (located where you start a new game).
*   **Reward:** If all 5 Cryptids are killed, speak to **Alfin** to receive the **Medal of Strength**.

#### Side Quest: Kreuzen for a Bruisin'
1.  Return to *Courageous*, speak to Towa, and set destination to **Celdic**.
2.  Go to **East Celdic Highway 2** -> **North Kreuzen Highway 3**.
3.  Find the quest monster at the **southwest end**.
    *   **Boss:** **Abearddon** (HP: 132,446).
    *   **Reward:** **Intimidation** Quartz, 3000 Mira, 4 AP.

### Shrine Trials Part A

You must revisit all Shrines. **Emma** must be in your party to access new trial areas. Shrines are marked with a red exclamation mark. Clear one to trigger a story event; the rest can be done later.

#### Terra Shrine - Trial
*   **Entrance:** Speak to **Beryl** to update her profile.
*   **Mechanics:**
    *   Use warp device. Emma unlocks the gate.
    *   **Wall 1:** Hit with someone having **Strike Proficiency B+** (Laura, Millium, Machias) to create a bridge.
    *   **Wall 2:** Hit near an enemy to create a bridge.
*   **Treasures:**
    *   Earth Sepith, Ancient Glyph, Tear All Balm, Genbu Quartz (Monster Chest), Earth Bell Quartz.
    *   **Trial Chests:** Alisa/Fie and Machias/Millium.
        *   *Boss (Alisa/Fie):* Minos Lord + Minos Demons. (Weak to Faint).
        *   *Boss (Machias/Millium):* Dino Slug + Dissolution Slugs. (Weak to Faint).
*   **End:** Hit the final wall, cross to the boss room.
    *   **Boss:** Magic Knight Dire Wolf (see "End of 1st Shrine").
    *   **Reward:** Zemurian Crystal.

#### Aria Shrine - Trial
*   **Mechanics:** Requires **Piecing Proficiency B+** (Alisa, Fie, Machias, Sara) for switches.
*   **Treasures:**
    *   Ragna Vortex, Wind Bell, Celestial Balm EX, Wind Sepith, Zeus Gem (Monster Chest).
    *   **Trial Chests:** Fie/Millium and Jusis/Sara.
        *   *Boss (Fie/Millium):* Clay Doll Omega. (Use Evasion Tank strategy).
        *   *Boss (Jusis/Sara):* Heavy Stalker + Stone Stalkers. (Use Faint resistance and Wind Arts).
*   **Important:** Do not leave without the **Zeus Gem** (essential for Final Dungeon).
*   **End:** Complete the bridge, reach the boss room.
    *   **Reward:** Zemurian Crystal.

#### Aqua Shrine - Trial
*   **Mechanics:** Requires **Thrust Proficiency B+** (Fie, Gaius, Jusis, Sara) to drain water.
*   **Treasures:**
    *   Maelstrom, Yaksha, Curia Balm, Water Bell, Water Sepith, EP Charge IV.
    *   **Trial Chests:** Alisa/Machias and Laura/Millium.
        *   *Boss (Laura/Millium):* Grand Ghastly + Gust Frogs. (High STR S-Crafts effective).
        *   *Boss (Alisa/Machias):* Gigantic Sharkodile. (Use Earth Pulse on Machias for regen).
*   **End:** Drain water, reach the boss room.
    *   **Reward:** Zemurian Crystal.

#### Ignis Shrine - Trial
*   **Mechanics:** Use **Rean's field attack** to extinguish flames and create bridges.
*   **Treasures:**
    *   Fire Sepith, Purgatorial Flame, Fire Bell, Carnage (Monster Chest).
    *   **Trial Chests:** Alisa/Sara and Machias/Emma.
        *   *Boss (Alisa/Sara):* Homuramikoto. (Impede to stop Flare Bomb; use Water Arts).
        *   *Boss (Machias/Emma):* Arch Geist. (Impede to stop Grim Butterfly; use Sleep/Nightmare resist).
*   **End:** Extinguish final flame, reach the boss room.
    *   **Reward:** Zemurian Crystal.

#### End of 1st Shrine
*   **Boss:** **Magic Knight Dire Wolf** (HP: 156,170).
    *   Uses Ground Punch, Ground Smash, and Boost (cures 23k HP + buffs stats).
    *   *Strategy:* Focus on self-buffing or arts. Avoid prolonged battles due to healing.
*   **Post-Boss:** Leave the Shrine, speak to **George** (1F Courageous), and select the red option to advance the story.

### December 24 & 25

*   **Fixed Party:** Jusis and Sara are fixed for the next segment.
*   **Events:** Survey key events in **Celdic**:
    1.  Chapel south room.
    2.  **Otto** (Manager House 2F).
    3.  **Claire** (Army Guardhouse entrance).
*   After all events, head to the **East exit of Celdic** for a cutscene.
*   **Back on Courageous:**
    *   Equip Valimar with best Ex-Orbs.
    *   Speak to **Fidelio** (Computer Room 2F), **Loggins** (Sword Training 4F), and **Linde** (Bridge 5F) to update profiles.
    *   Speak to **Towa** to start the operation.

#### Boss: Kestrel
*   **HP:** 260,000.
*   **Strategy:** Similar to Spiegel. Save CP for counters or Seal (Last CP ability) when it switches stances.

#### Post-Boss Event
*   Fight a group of Jaegers with **Instructor Sara** only.
*   **Sara's S-Craft:** Use it when CP is full.
*   Proceed to **Aurochs Fort**.

#### Aurochs Fort
*   **Navigation:**
    *   **1F:** North path -> East -> South room (Curia Balm). Southwest (Cast 2). Northeast room (Celestial Balm EX). Air duct -> Southeast area (Hellfire Quartz).
    *   **2F:** North path -> Center gate -> Air duct (Chrono Burst Quartz). Southwest duct -> Southeast corner (Tearal Quartz). Northwest duct -> North section (HP 2 Quartz).
    *   **3F:** North path -> Northeast room (U-Materials).
*   **Boss:** **Duvalie** (HP: 203,010) + **Sleipnir x2**.
    *   *Strategy:* Kill Sleipnirs first with S-Crafts. Duvalie uses Prism Calibur (10k-13k dmg) below 60% HP. Use **Platinum Shield** (Jusis) or Adamantine Shield.

    *   Speak to **Maicel** (table) -> Receive *Gambler Jack II - Chapter 12*.
*   **Duke's Mansion:**
    *   Event scene in the main hall.
    *   West room event.
