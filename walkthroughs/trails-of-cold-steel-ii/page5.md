The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
Intermission Chapter

After you have finished the event scene , visit each room. They are all marked with a red exclamation mark on the map.

Scarlet - South Area (Southeast Room)
Vulcan - South Area (Northeast Room)
Bleublanc - Dining Table (Center)
Xeno & Leonidas - North Area (Southeast Room)
McBurn - North Area (Northeast Room)
Duvalie - North Area (Southwest Room)
Altina - North Area (Northwest Room)
After you have spoken to all the everyone listed above, move to the second floor and go north to the guest room. Knock on the door. Watch the event, after this scene, Rean will get the Spirit Unification ability. Head downstairs and go to your room (South Area, Southwest corner). Examine the air ducts by the flower vase. Go all the way to the end, after this, you will be facing a few battles.

Best thing to do is use Alfin's arts, it should deal enough damage to destroy them in three blows. You should cancel the Soldier's casting with Rean's Autumn Leaf Cutter when the opportunity comes. When Rean's HP drops very low, use Alfin to heal him.

After defeating the soldiers, continue along the linear path. You'll come across two rooms. Go to the room to the west to find a Celestial Balm, and the room to the east for an Orange Cape. Return to the main path and you'll encounter another group of soldiers. Defeat them and continue. When you reach the Orbment Charging Station at the southern end, make sure to heal up and enter the room for an upcoming boss fight.
