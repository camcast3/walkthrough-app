# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
## Intermission Chapter

After you have finished the event scene, visit each room. They are all marked with a **red exclamation mark** on the map.

### Characters to Visit
- **Scarlet** – South Area (Southeast Room)
- **Vulcan** – South Area (Northeast Room)
- **Bleublanc** – Dining Table (Center)
- **Xeno & Leonidas** – North Area (Southeast Room)
- **McBurn** – North Area (Northeast Room)
- **Duvalie** – North Area (Southwest Room)
- **Altina** – North Area (Northwest Room)

---

### Spirit Unification & Air Ducts
After speaking with everyone listed above:
1. Move to the **second floor** and go **north** to the guest room.
2. Knock on the door and watch the event.
3. **Rean** will obtain the **Spirit Unification** ability.
4. Head downstairs to your room (**South Area, Southwest corner**).
5. Examine the **air ducts** by the flower vase.
6. Proceed to the end to face a series of battles.

### Final Boss: Crow
*Location: Final room of the chapter*

| Stat | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 96,640 |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | Earth: 90%, Water: 90%, Fire: 90%, Wind: 90% |
| **Unbalance Efficacy** | Slash: ★☆☆☆, Thrust: ☆☆☆☆, Pierce: ☆☆☆☆, Strike: ☆☆☆☆ |

**Strategy:**
- Exchange attacks with Crow.
- If you see a **turn order bonus** (e.g., Critical), use your **S-Craft** immediately.
- **Warning:** When Crow's HP drops below **50%**, ensure your HP is **higher than 7,000**. He may use his S-Craft, which can instantly kill you if your HP is too low.
