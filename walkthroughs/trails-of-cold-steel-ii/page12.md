## Hidden & Story Quests

After finishing the three quests listed above, **Neithardt** will call you. Go meet him at the **Main Building Faculty Lounge**.

### Hidden Quest: Swimming Lessons: The Return
Speak to **Instructor Neithardt** and accept his request to participate in the swimming mini-game.
*   You can choose two opponents (one boy, one girl of Class VII). This choice increases **Link EXP** with them, so pick someone you still need levels with.
*   **Mechanic:** Press button prompts when rings are highlighted to increase swimming speed (same as the first Cold Steel game).
*   **Bonus:** Beating your opponent earns an extra **1 AP**.

*   **Client:** Instructor Neithardt
*   **Reward:** Zeram Capsule, 300 Link EXP, 3000 Mira, 1 + (1) AP

### Story Quest: The Race to End All Races
Head to the **Engineering Building** and speak with either **Angelica** or **George** to begin.
*   You will be challenged to a race against Angelica.
*   **Winning:** Hold down the accelerate button, get ahead, and block Angelica. Maintain the lead.
*   **Bonus:** Defeating her earns **1 AP**. If you lose, you can rematch for the AP bonus.

*   **Client:** Angelica
*   **Reward:** Lightning Belt, EP Charge IV x3, Engineering Building Key, 3000 Mira, 1 + (1) AP

### Story Quest: Class Warfare / 実技テストの総仕上げ [Required]
Talk to **Patrick** at the Academy Field. You will fight him in a 2v2 battle.

**Battle 1: Patrick & Celestin**
*   **Strategy:** Inflict Delay and status deficits. When HP is critical, Celestin may cast Seraphic Ring (heal). Using S-Crafts here grants 100 CP for the next battle.
*   **Weakness:** Both are weak to status ailments.

**Battle 2: Vincent, Ferris, & Sariffa (3v3)**
*   **Strategy:** Focus on **Sariffa** first (she freezes/petrifies and has high evasion). Then Vincent. Ferris supports with Crescent Mirror.
*   **Weakness:** Status ailments work well.

**Battle 3: Vandyck & Beatrice**
*   **Strategy:** Save S-Crafts and Overdrive. They are immune to status ailments, but **AT Delay** works. Vandyck is the main attacker (high damage); Beatrice heals.
*   **Key:** Use AT Delay to stop Vandyck. If hit, heal immediately with Seraphic Ring.

*   **Client:** Patrick
*   **Reward:** Albion Garb, Tear All Balm x3, Link Exp +200, 3000 Mira, 1 + (1) AP

> **Note:** If playing the Japanese 1.00 version, update the game to avoid crashes when speaking to Patrick.

After the story quest, move to a random location for an event scene, then go to the **Old School House** to continue the main story.

## Reverie Corridor

The **Reverie Corridor** is the true final dungeon, special for the characters it unlocks, the mechanics it offers, and the unique items it provides.

### List of Features

*   **All Playable Characters:** Access to 18 regular party members (Class VII + Guests) and 5 Special characters.
*   **Increased Link EXP:** Gain **3x** Link EXP after fights, helping to close gaps between characters.
*   **Regenerating Chests:** Loot resets upon re-entering the dungeon.
*   **Valimar:** Available for all boss fights.
*   **Zemurian Ore:** Earn four Zemurian Ore Shards by defeating the four sector bosses, allowing you to craft another Ultimate Weapon.

### Dungeon Mechanics

*   **Four Sectors:** The dungeon consists of four sectors themed around basic elements:
    *   **Yellow:** Earth
    *   **Red:** Fire
    *   **Blue:** Water
    *   **Green:** Wind
    *   *Note:* Enemies have resistances and weaknesses based on the sector's element.
*   **Semi-Random Layout:** The order and layout of sectors change **every time** you leave and re-enter the Old School House.
*   **Order-Specific Enemies:**
    *   **Type & Strength:** Sector 1 contains small, stunnable enemies. Sector 4 contains 90% "Tough" enemies that cannot be stunned. Difficulty increases as you progress.
    *   **Availability:** Enemy types depend on the sector order. For example, a Fire sector appearing before an Earth sector may spawn unique enemies.
*   **Treasure Regeneration:** Chests regenerate content every time you re-enter the dungeon.
    *   **Brown Chests:** Sepith, Monster Ingredients, high-level curatives (Zeram Powders/Capsules).
    *   **Red Chests:** Accessories and Lv 3 Quartz.
    *   **Monster Chests:** Special Quartz (Dragon Vision, Genbu, Suzaku, 'Gem', and 'Bell' types).
    *   *Note:* Loot quality improves in Sectors 3 and 4.
*   **Phantasmal Mirrors:** Special accessories that unlock the use of Special Characters.

### Playable Characters

**Guest Characters:**
Elise, Alfin, Towa, Angelica, Toval, Claire, and Sharon join as regular party members.
*   **1st Playthrough Levels:**
    *   Elise & Alfin: **Lv 115**
    *   Toval, Claire, Sharon: **Lv 130**
    *   Towa & Angelica: Level from end of Chapter 2 Act 4.

### Phantasmal Mirrors & Special Characters

Purchase **Phantasmal Mirrors** at the Orbment Station for **200,000 Mira each**. When equipped, they allow a party member to substitute into battle as a Special Character.

**Available Mirrors:**
*   **Male Only:**
    *   Azure Knight (Crow)
    *   Burning Bravery (Lloyd)
*   **Female Only:**
    *   Witch of the Abyss (Vita)
    *   Black Rabbit (Altina)
    *   Silver Moon Princess (Rixia)

**Special Character Mechanics:**
*   **No Setup Screens:** Special characters cannot have their equipment or quartz changed; they do not have Status, Orbment, or Equip screens.
*   **Battle Only:** They only appear in battle, substituting the character wearing the corresponding mirror.
*   **Dynamic Levels:** Their level and stats **depend entirely** on the character wearing the mirror. The mirror replaces the user's stats, palette, quartz set, and abilities.
*   **EXP Distribution:** All EXP and Link EXP goes to the character wearing the mirror. The Special Character itself does not level up.
*   **Outside Combat:** They have no relevance outside the Old School House.
*   **Passive Buffs:** Each Special Character provides the effects of the **Ice Crown** accessory (permanent 10 CP Regeneration and Critical Up) and includes a full set of **Lost Arts** and a **Level 5 Master Quartz**.

### Final Boss Strategy

The dungeon is randomized, but remember:
*   **Cryptids:** Fought at the end of every 4th floor.
*   **True Final Boss:** Fought on the **17th floor**.

> **Tip:** Sell all your Sepith and spend all your Mira before finishing the game to avoid wasting points in New Game+.
> **Note:** In a 2nd Playthrough, defeating the final boss with a Phantasmal Mirror character unlocks a bonus scene.

#### Boss Stats: Loa Luciferia
| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 722,250 (Normal) |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | Earth 80, Water 80, Fire 80, Wind 80, Time 100, Space 100, Mirage 100 |
| **Weaknesses** | None (Immune to AT Delay) |
| **Resistances** | ★☆☆☆ (Slash, Thrust, Pierce, Strike) |

#### Preparation
*   **Formation:** Baiting strategy (1 or 2 characters) or 2 characters per strategy.
*   **Quartz:**
    *   **Defense:** Adamantine Shield, Earth Bell, Aegis MQ, Aries MQ.
    *   **Offense:** Shadow Blade, Altair Cannon, Claiomh Solarion.
    *   **Healers:** Must have **Seraphic Ring**, **Insight**, and **Impassion** (unless using Alisa).
    *   **Millium:** Raven MQ and Quick Feet Set.
*   **Accessories:** Dark Pendulum/Emblem, Vanish, Death, and Stat Down protection.
*   **Links:** Swap links constantly to maintain Overdrive.

**Suggested Party:**
*   Alisa, Fie, Alfin
*   'Vita' (Phantasmal Mirror - Witch of the Abyss)
*   Laura
*   Millium/Altina (Phantasmal Mirror - Black Rabbit)
*   Jusis
*   'Lloyd' (Phantasmal Mirror - Burning Bravery)
*   Rixia (Phantasmal Mirror - Silver Moon Princess)

#### Strategies

**1. Baiting Strategy**
*   **Execution:** Move Fie, Millium, Altina, or Lloyd to attack immediately after the boss's first turn.
*   **Lloyd:** Use **Burning Heart II** before attacking.
*   **Fie:** Cast **Insight** immediately and maintain a constant **Spd+ 50%** buff. This ensures the boss misses all attacks and cannot "suck" her in.
*   **Millium:** Buff with **Spd+ 50%**, **Impassion** (or True Gladiator Belt), and **Insight**. Cast **Ultimate Barrier** every single turn.
*   **Altina:** Similar to Millium but faster. Attack physically if possible or apply her Spd buff to herself.
*   **Lloyd:** Since he is the fastest with Burning Heart II, he can attack physically or use **Brave Smash II** on the boss and minions. Apply **Insight** to all party members immediately as the boss's craft hits the whole field and negates buffs.
*   **Damage Dealer:** Use a Mage (Vita, Emma, Elliot, Rixia) as the main damage dealer.

**2. S-Craft Strategy**
*   **Opening:** Immediately use **Alfin's Sacred Circle**, **Jusis' Platinum Shield**, or **Adamantine Shield** (with Lv 5 Aegis/Earth Bell).
*   **Buffing:** Use Overdrive to buff the party, ensuring everyone has **Insight**.
*   **Aoe:** Have a Mage target the whole battlefield with all-hitting arts or use Vita's crafts to clear summoned mobs.
*   **Finishing:** Build CP and blast the boss as soon as it hits 100 CP. Any Special Character used here becomes the main damage dealer.
*   **2nd Playthrough Tip:** Give **Laura** or **Rean** the **Ice Crown**.

#### Fight Analysis
*   **Immunity:** Loa Luciferia is immune to **AT Delay**.
*   **Speed:** The boss is faster than your characters at all times.
*   **S-Craft:** The boss uses its S-Craft as an opening move and throughout the fight. It cannot be blocked by shields, hits for **1 HP** on all characters, and removes status buffs.
*   **Insight:** Essential for dodging the S-Craft. Shields protect those who do not dodge.
*   **Absorb:** If a party member is absorbed, use **S-Break** immediately unless a Mage can attack the boss on the next turn.
*   **Best Character:** **Vita** is highly recommended due to her power, HP/EP regeneration with offensive arts, useful status effects (Summon Grianos), and natural resistance to Vanish.
*   **Crow Note:** While fast, Crow's attacks deal less damage and his "Criminal Edge" pushes mobs away from the boss, hindering mages using Shadow Blade. He can replace Laura for high damage with Deadly Cross.
*   **Damage:** With Domination Quartz and full buffs, you can deal **180,000 to 350,000** damage per hit.

Sit back, watch the ending, and keep your save data for *Trails of Cold Steel III*.
*   **Memories:** After completion, the **Memories** option appears in the system menu.
*   **Final Bonding:** If on New Game+, watch the Final Bonding event on December 30 and save to unlock it in Memories.
