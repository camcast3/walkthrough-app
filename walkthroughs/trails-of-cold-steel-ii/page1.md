Prologue Part A: November 29
Start a new game and select the game's difficulty level. If you plan on getting the Platinum trophy, you will need at least two playthroughs. If you have a save data from Trails of Cold Steel, you can transfer your save file over. This will let you start a new game with some extra bonuses.

Watch the introduction sequence, if you haven't beaten the original Trails of Cold Steel, this part will give you a summary of the ending of the original game, so beware of the spoilers you are about to see. After the intro sequences is over, you will get to control Rean.

Enemy information in this guide is based on Normal mode circumstances. As you claim control, the game will let you know about saving. This is done by going to the menu and using the Save option. You can also access the Cross-Save feature, which lets you upload your save data to PSN. This is for players to transfer save files between the PS3 or Vita.

If you ever get lost, pressing the Square button will open the Navigation menu, it will also highlight your next objective on the bottom right of the screen.

Head downhill, you will notice a shining object. Examine it to receive Rean's equipment. The game will let you know about the Orbment system. Go to the menu and equip your Master quartz under the Orbment section. Now run down the hill, you will see a monster, by touching it, you will engage in battle. If you manage to sneak up on a monster, or stun it with your field attack, (Pressing the Confirm button, X or O in Japanese version) you will get an additional advantage during battle. The bonus includes extra turns and reduced enemy HP. After you have defeated the monster in combat, the game will let you know about Monster Notes. Each time you defeated a monster, additional data is filled in regarding to enemies you have previously fought. If you want to fill in this information immediately you need to use the Battle Scope item, a quartz with the spell "Analyze" or Emma and Eliot's Detector ability. Now just keep going east to see another event. Then further down you will fight a rock type creature. The game will prompt you into the basics of Arts. This monster has a high physical defense, so use the Fire Bolt spell to quickly defeat the monster. Now further down the hill, Rean will notice an Orbment Charging Station, examine it to fully restore your character's HP and EP. Now enter the next area to Eisengard Mountains 2.

Eisengard Range

Head down the hill to find a treasure chest that contains a Tear quartz. Go a little further, Rean will notice it's a strong type of monster. Fight it, the game will let you know about using the S-Craft function. S-Crafts are super attacks that require a 100-200 CP to perform, found under the Craft section. It can also be used by pressing PSTRIANGLE.png Triangle when it's not your turn, although using it this way adds a lot more delay to your turn.

When you have defeated the monster, head to the south corner of the map to find a Teara Balm. Then go to the east area for an event scene. Then head to the northeast corner of the map to find a Proxy Puppet in the treasure box. Then take the southeast exit to Eisengard Mountain 3. Pick up the Celestial Balm at the treasure box just directly south and then go all the way to the south end to find an Orbment Charging Station. Heal yourself and go to the southeast end to fight a boss battle.

Note: Maps in this guide are designed with the compass facing north.

The first boss battle will be pretty basic. All the boss does is it attacks with its sword. Use Flame Strike to hurt the boss, this ability has a chance to inflict Burn, this will help you expedite his demise. Heal when Rean is under 3000 HP with either Tear or Teara Balm. Prologue Part B: November 30
Ymir
Trails of cold steel ii ymir.jpg

Watch the event scene, you will be introduced to Elise, Alfin and Toval again. Afterwards, leave the building, the game will let you know about events. story events are marked with a red exclamation mark and a green exclamation mark for side events. Then go to the general store "Plover" located at the southwest corner of Ymir. Purchase the Imperial Chronicle Issue #1 and Gambler Jack Reprint Chapter 1 (There is a trophy for collecting all books found in the game).

Now visit all the red exclamation mark areas marked on the map.

Rean's Parents (Lucia and Baron Schwarzer) at the Baron's Mansion
Elise and Celine at the Footbath
(This will add the friends note on the menu. Speaking to characters at the right moment will add a page to their profiles, collect them all for a bronze trophy.)
Toval at the Inn & Pub "Valley Echo" (You can also speak to pub owner, Gerald for some pancakes.)
Werner at the kitchen area, of the Hotel "The Phoenix Wings" (Northeast on map)
(This will add the recipe section to the menu and a new recipe.)
Princess Alfin at the Septian Church
Annabelle at Ymir Canyon
(Annabelle will give you a fishing rod and the fishing section will be added to the menu. If you have a save data from the original Cold Steel, Annabelle will give you some Groundbait depending on your fishing rank. You can also speak to Annabelle to exchange prizes)
When your done doing all the activities listed above. Go to the Phoenix Wing hotel and speak to the receptionist, Baggins and then go to Hot Springs on right side of the building. Entering the Hot Spring will automatically advance the story, so finished everything you need to do before going in. Afterwards, Elise and Toval will join your party. Go back inside the Baron Mansion and speak to Alfin for a short event scene. You can also use the footbath at the center of town to fully restore your party, including CP as well. When you are all ready to go, head to the northwest exit to go to Ymir Canyon.

Ezgif-5-e961afaae2.gif Fresh Nightmare Mode Recommendations
Before advancing in this area, it's recommended to stock up on Teara and Reviving Balms. To fund this, you can cook Salty Onigiri with Elise to create Tri-Colored Onigiri (Superb result) and then sell them for 150 Mira each. You should aim to have at least 15 to 20 of each. Additionally, ensure that all three characters are equipped with the best accessory available from the store, which boosts Strength, ATS, and Speed. Finally, invest in EP Charges with any remaining funds.
Ymir Canyon
Watch the event scene, when you the first monster, the game will let you know about Link attacks. Hitting enemies with the right attack type can cause an enemy to unbalance which will lead to Link attack for bonus damage. Now finish off the first monster and continue on. Go to the northeast end to find a treasure chest that contains the Lighter accessory. Continue on through the path northwest, if you reach to the northwest end, you will find a monster chest that contains a Zeram Powder. You can use Toval's S-Craft on kill all eight enemies at once to get the Pieces of Eight trophy. Then go back south and cross the bridge to the east, pick up the U-Material in the chest located at the east end. Then head to the north exit to Ymir Canyon 2.

Pick up the Tearal Balm on your way north, then take the northwest path, eventually the path will split, by going south you can find a Poison Quartz in the treasure box. Then go north, all the way to end. You will find another chest with some Sepith and just a little further will be an Orbment Charging Station. Heal up and enter the next area for a boss fight.

You will be using the Ashen Knight, Valimar for this battle. Mech combat is like playing a game of rock-paper-scissors. You need to select a body part and hit. Attacking the correct part will land a critical hit which allows you to perform a Link Attack. Link Attacks provide damage bonus, making them the optimal method to win. Use the picture below as a reference on which part to attack. There isn't much strategy to this battle, just attack it's head during it's idle stance, and it's arms during it's attack stance.
