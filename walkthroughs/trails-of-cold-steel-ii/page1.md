## Prologue Part A: November 29

Start a new game and select the game's difficulty level. If you plan on getting the Platinum trophy, you will need at least two playthroughs. If you have a save data from *Trails of Cold Steel*, you can transfer your save file over. This will let you start a new game with some extra bonuses.

### Difficulty Settings

| Difficulty | Description | Stats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Easy** | Balanced for quick, easy battles. Ideal for beginners and story enthusiasts. | Enemies are at 0.70 of their stats. |
| **Normal** | Build around satisfyingly tough combat. Aimed to people used to playing RPGs. | Enemies are at 1.00 of their stats. |
| **Hard** | Battles will take everything you have. For skilled players who find Normal too easy. | Enemies are at 1.15 of their stats. |
| **Nightmare** | Balanced for New Game+. Ideal for replays. | Enemies are at 1.30 of their stats. |

> **Note:** Watch the introduction sequence carefully. If you haven't beaten the original *Trails of Cold Steel*, this part will give you a summary of the ending of the original game, so **beware of the spoilers** you are about to see.

After the intro sequence is over, you will get to control **Rean**. Enemy information in this guide is based on **Normal mode** circumstances.

As you claim control, the game will let you know about **saving**. This is done by going to the menu and using the **Save** option. You can also access the **Cross-Save feature**, which lets you upload your save data to PSN. This is for players to transfer save files between the PS3 or Vita.

If you ever get lost, pressing the **Square** button will open the **Navigation menu**; it will also highlight your next objective on the bottom right of the screen.

### Gameplay Walkthrough

1.  **Start:** Head downhill. You will notice a shining object. Examine it to receive Rean's equipment.
2.  **Orbment:** The game will let you know about the **Orbment** system. Go to the menu and equip your Master quartz under the Orbment section.
3.  **First Battle:** Run down the hill. You will see a monster; by touching it, you will engage in battle.
    *   *Tip:* If you manage to sneak up on a monster, or stun it with your field attack (Pressing the Confirm button, **X** or **O** in Japanese version), you will get an additional **advantage** during battle (extra turns and reduced enemy HP).
4.  **Monster Notes:** After defeating the monster, the game introduces **Monster Notes**. Each time you defeat a monster, additional data is filled in regarding enemies you have previously fought.
    *   *Tip:* To fill in this information immediately, use the **Battle Scope** item, a quartz with the spell **"Analyze"**, or **Emma and Eliot's Detector ability**.
5.  **Arts:** Continue east to see an event, then fight a rock-type creature. The game prompts you into the basics of **Arts**. This monster has high physical defense, so use the **Fire Bolt** spell to quickly defeat it.
6.  **Healing:** Further down the hill, Rean will notice an **Orbment Charging Station**. Examine it to fully restore your character's HP and EP.
7.  **Next Area:** Enter the next area to **Eisengard Mountains 2**.

#### Eisengard Range

*   Head down the hill to find a treasure chest containing a **Tear** quartz.
*   Go a little further; Rean will notice a strong type of monster. Fight it.
    *   *Mechanic:* The game introduces the **S-Craft** function. S-Crafts are super attacks that require 100-200 CP to perform (found under the Craft section). It can also be used by pressing **Triangle** when it's not your turn, although this adds a lot more delay to your turn.
*   **Loot:**
    *   South corner of the map: **Teara Balm**.
    *   East area: Event scene.
    *   Northeast corner: **Proxy Puppet** (treasure box).
*   **Path:** Take the southeast exit to **Eisengard Mountain 3**.
    *   Pick up the **Celestial Balm** at the treasure box directly south.
    *   Go to the south end to find an **Orbment Charging Station**. Heal yourself.
    *   Go to the southeast end to fight a boss battle.

> **Note:** Maps in this guide are designed with the compass facing north.

### Boss: Magic Knight Ortheim

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 33,768 |
| **Item Drop** | None |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | 100% (Earth, Water, Fire, Wind) |
| **Unbalance Efficacy** | Slash: ★★★☆, Thrust: ★★★☆, Pierce: ★★★☆, Strike: ★★★☆ |

**Strategy:**
The first boss battle is pretty basic. The boss attacks with its sword.
*   **Attack:** Use **Flame Strike** to hurt the boss; this ability has a chance to inflict **Burn**, which will help expedite its demise.
*   **Healing:** Heal when Rean is under 3000 HP with either **Tear** or **Teara Balm**. If the boss gets 2 turns in a row, use a **Teara Balm** immediately.
*   **Victory:** As soon as you've brought it under 15,000 HP, the battle will end.

## Prologue Part B: November 30

### Ymir

Watch the event scene; you will be introduced to **Elise**, **Alfin**, and **Toval** again. Afterwards, leave the building. The game introduces **events**:
*   **Story events** are marked with a **red exclamation mark**.
*   **Side events** are marked with a **green exclamation mark**.

#### Activities in Ymir
1.  **General Store "Plover"** (Southwest corner):
    *   Purchase **Imperial Chronicle Issue #1** and **Gambler Jack Reprint Chapter 1**.
    *   *Note:* There is a trophy for collecting all books found in the game.
2.  **Visit Red Exclamation Mark Areas:**
    *   **Rean's Parents** (Lucia and Baron Schwarzer) at the **Baron's Mansion**.
    *   **Elise and Celine** at the **Footbath** (Adds "Friends Note" to menu; collect profile pages for a bronze trophy).
    *   **Toval** at the **Inn & Pub "Valley Echo"** (Speak to pub owner Gerald for pancakes).
    *   **Werner** at the kitchen area of the Hotel **"The Phoenix Wings"** (Northeast on map).
        *   *Reward:* Adds the recipe section to the menu and a new recipe.
    *   **Princess Alfin** at the **Septian Church**.
    *   **Annabelle** at **Ymir Canyon**:
        *   Gives a fishing rod; adds fishing section to menu.
        *   If you have a save from the original *Cold Steel*, she gives Groundbait based on rank.
        *   Can exchange prizes.

#### Progression
When done with activities:
1.  Go to the **Phoenix Wing** hotel and speak to the receptionist, **Baggins**.
2.  Go to the **Hot Springs** on the right side of the building.
    *   *Warning:* Entering the Hot Spring automatically advances the story. Finish everything else first.
3.  **Elise and Toval** will join your party.
4.  Go back inside the **Baron Mansion** and speak to **Alfin** for a short event scene.
5.  Use the **footbath** at the center of town to fully restore your party, including CP.
6.  Head to the **northwest exit** to go to **Ymir Canyon**.

#### Fresh Nightmare Mode Recommendations
Before advancing in Ymir Canyon (if playing on Nightmare or preparing for difficulty):
*   **Stock Up:** Aim for 15–20 **Teara Balms** and **Reviving Balms**.
*   **Farming:** Cook```markdown
**Salty Onigiri** with Elise to create **Tri-Colored Onigiri** (Superb result) and sell for 150 Mira each.
*   **Equipment:** Ensure all three characters are equipped with the best accessory from the store (boosts Strength, ATS, and Speed).
*   **Consumables:** Invest remaining funds in **EP Charges**.

### Ymir Canyon

Watch the event scene. When you encounter the first monster, the game introduces **Link attacks**:
*   Hitting enemies with the right attack type can cause them to unbalance, triggering a **Link attack** for bonus damage.
*   Finish off the first monster and continue.

#### Treasure & Exploration
*   **Northeast end:** Treasure chest containing the **Lighter** accessory.
*   **Northwest path:** Reach the northwest end to find a monster chest with **Zeram Powder**.
    *   *Trophy:* Use **Toval's S-Craft** to kill all eight enemies at once to get the **Pieces of Eight** trophy.
*   **Backtrack:** Go back south, cross the bridge to the east.
    *   Pick up **U-Material** in the chest at the east end.
*   **North exit:** Head to **Ymir Canyon 2**.

#### Ymir Canyon 2
*   Pick up the **Tearal Balm** on your way north.
*   Take the northwest path. The path splits:
    *   **South:** Find a **Poison Quartz** in the treasure box.
    *   **North:** Go all the way to the end.
        *   Find a chest with **Sepith** (All Colors x50).
        *   Just a little further: **Orbment Charging Station**. Heal up.
        *   Enter the next area for a boss fight.

### Boss: Magic Knight Ortheim (Ymir Canyon)

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 45,225 |
| **Item Drop** | None |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | 100% (Earth, Water, Fire, Wind) |
| **Unbalance Efficacy** | Slash: ★★☆☆, Thrust: ★★☆☆, Pierce: ★★☆☆, Strike: ★★☆☆ |

**Boss Skills:**
*   **Sword Slash:** Hits everyone in front.
*   **Ground Stab:** Targets area around boss (L).
*   **Boost:** Grants boss **STR 50% Up** (Red) and cures **15,000 HP** (normal).

**Strategy:**
Ortheim has a massive upgrade compared to the previous encounter.
*   **Area Attack:** Can hit hard; be prepared.
*   **Buff:** When Ortheim buffs itself, it raises attack power and restores 20% of its HP — prolonging the fight.
*   **Healer:** Use **Elise** as your main healer. She can use **Holy Squall** (restores 60% HP in small radius) or **Tear**.
*   **DPS:** Use **Rean** and **Toval** as main attackers. Rely on physical attacks and use your **S-Craft** on turns with a critical bonus.

### Boss: Magic Knight Ortheim (Phase 2) — Ashen Knight, Valimar

| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 84,400 |
| **Item Drop** | None |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | 100% (Earth, Water, Fire, Wind) |

**Stances & Weak Points:**

| Stance | Body | Head | Arms |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Idle Stance** | ★★★☆ | ★★★★ | ★☆☆☆ |
| **Attack Stance** | ★★☆☆ | ★☆☆☆ | ★★★★ |

**Strategy:**
Mech combat is like playing **rock-paper-scissors**. You need to select a body part and hit.
*   **Critical Hits:** Attacking the correct part lands a critical hit, allowing you to perform a **Link Attack** (damage bonus).
*   **Phase 2 Strategy:**
    *   **Idle Stance:** Attack the **Head**.
    *   **Attack Stance:** Attack the **Arms**.
*   There isn't much strategy — just follow the pattern above to win.
