December 1st
As soon as you gain control of Rean, you'll notice the addition of the ability to Quick Travel under the navigation menu. Your first task is to head to the general store and speak to Raio, the shopkeeper on the right. The game will introduce you to weapon synthesis and the process of unlocking Orbment Slots. The Orbment system involves upgrading slots to equip more potent types of Quartz.

Next, speak to Camillia. Watching the event scene with her will unlock the item trading function, similar to the pawn shop feature from the previous game. (Note: If this is your second playthrough, she will allow you to trade Zemurian Ore Shards for 50 U-Materials.)

Before leaving town through Ymir Canyon, don't forget to speak to Luke near the wreckage of the station. He'll provide you with some Tear Balms to aid you on your journey. Once you're ready, it's time to venture out.

Lunaria Nature Park

After the event scene, you will be warped into Lunaria Nature Park. Head south to Lunaria Nature Park 3. The game will let you know that this area contains high elements, which means Time, Space, and Mirage elements have effects on monsters here, there is also an additional type of bonus effects on turn order which can be an advantage or disadvantage for your party. Head down to the southeast corner to pick up a Stone Brooch in a treasure chest, then continue all the way to the southwest path. On the very southwest corner of the map contains some Sepith Mass in the treasure box. Then head to the exit located southeast of Lunaria Nature Park 2.

The path will then split into two ways, head to the west end to get a Teara Balm from the treasure box, and there is also a fishing spot near this area. Afterwards, go to the east end to get Citrus Bottle from the treasure chest. Then take the bridge south and go all the way to the west corner to find a Scepter Master Quartz in a monster chest. Then head to the opposite end, by the east area for an EP Charge II. After that go to the south exit of the map to go to Lunaria Nature Park 1.

Head south, and go to the east side of the map to the northeast corner to find an HP 1 in a treasure chest, then head south and go all the way to the southwest corner to get some Sepith. Then they the center path south, when you're near the end of the park, you will see an Orbment Charging Station. Leave the park to face a boss battle.

As soon as this battle starts, the game will let you know about the Overdrive function. This function is highlighted through the small circle gauge at the upper right part of the screen. Using Overdrive will restore that character's HP, EP, CP, granting them three extra turns, and an instant magic cast between the two characters that are linked. This is a very powerful function that can be used to exploit bosses. Anyways activate Overdrive by highlighting the attack icon and Holding the Right button when it changes icon, and press the confirm button to do Overdrive. Buff up your characters and attack the boss. The boss has a few abilities, from buffing itself to paralyzing your characters with Seal or hitting a single target really hard. I recommend using Toval's wind magic against this boss, as he can cast them really quickly. Have Rean uses Autumn Leaf Cutter or Arc Slash to delay the boss. If your CP is maxed, save your S-Craft when the Boss's HP is low.

Once you have control over Rean, the game will let you know about bonding events. Bonding events are highlighted with a yellow exclamation mark on the map. Bonding events will reveal additional backstory for the character you are attending and improve your relationship with them. To attend a Bonding Event, you need Bonding Points. You are only given a limited amount of Bonding Points, which makes it impossible to see all of them on the first playthrough. However, you can get additional Bonding Points on New Game+ playthroughs.

Attend the bonding events, doing Machias and Claire's bonding event will add a page to their profiles.

QuesticonCS2.gif Hidden Quest: Leaf it to Me

To initiate this quest, interact with either Alf or Kiki, they're the two kids near the station. Choose the first dialogue option to offer your assistance. After accepting the quest, exit out of town and head to Ymir Valley.

In Ymir Valley, you need to find two different types of colored leaves for Alf and Kiki. The first leaf is located at the northwest corner of Ymir Valley 1. The second leaf can be found in the upper southeast corner of Ymir Valley 2, near a treasure chest. Once you have collected both leaves, return to Alf and Kiki to complete the quest.

Please note that there is a monkey-like monster in Ymir Valley, so be sure to scan it if you want to complete the Battle Trophy.
