### December 1st

As soon as you gain control of **Rean**, you will notice the addition of **Quick Travel** under the navigation menu.

1.  **General Store**: Head to the general store and speak to **Raio** (shopkeeper on the right).
    *   *Learn*: Weapon synthesis and unlocking Orbment Slots.
    *   *Note*: The Orbment system involves upgrading slots to equip more potent Quartz.
2.  **Camillia**: Speak to Camillia. Watching the event scene unlocks the **Item Trading** function (similar to a pawn shop).
    *   *Note*: If this is a second playthrough, she allows trading Zemurian Ore Shards for 50 U-Materials.
3.  **Station**: Before leaving town through Ymir Canyon, speak to **Luke** near the wreckage.
    *   *Rewards*: He provides some **Tear Balms**.

#### Lunaria Nature Park
After the event scene, you will be warped into **Lunaria Nature Park**. Head south to **Lunaria Nature Park 3**.

*   **Mechanic**: This area contains **High Elements** (Time, Space, and Mirage). These elements affect monsters and turn order (can be an advantage or disadvantage).

**Lunaria Nature Park 3**
*   Southeast corner: **Stone Brooch** (Treasure Chest).
*   Southwest corner: **Sepith Mass x100** (Treasure Box).
*   Exit: Southeast to **Lunaria Nature Park 2**.

**Lunaria Nature Park 2**
*   West end: **Teara Balm** (Treasure Box) + Fishing Spot nearby.
*   East end: **Citrus Bottle** (Treasure Chest).
*   Bridge South -> West corner: **Scepter** (Master Quartz) (Monster Chest).
*   East area: **EP Charge II**.
*   Exit: South to **Lunaria Nature Park 1**.

**Lunaria Nature Park 1**
*   Northeast corner (East side): **HP 1** (Treasure Chest).
*   Southwest corner: **Sepith x100** (Treasure).
*   Center path South: **Orbment Charging Station**.
*   **Boss Battle**: Leave the park to face **Grunoja**.

> **Boss: Grunoja**
> *   **HP**: 21,800
> *   **Item Drop**: Attack 2
> *   **Elemental Efficacy**: Earth (120%), Water (100%), Fire (140%), Wind/Time/Space/Mirage (100%)
> *   **Strategy**:
>     *   **Overdrive**: Activate by highlighting the attack icon, holding the Right button when it changes icon, and pressing Confirm. It restores HP, EP, CP, grants 3 extra turns, and allows instant magic casts between linked characters.
>     *   Buff up characters and attack. The boss can buff itself, paralyze with Seal, or hit hard.
>     *   **Recommendation**: Use Toval's wind magic. Have Rean use *Autumn Leaf Cutter* or *Arc Slash* to delay the boss. If CP is maxed, save your S-Craft for when the boss's HP is low.

#### West Celdic Highway & East Trista Highway
After defeating the boss:
1.  Leave the park and enter the farmhouse on the east side. Speak to residents for an event.
2.  Head south. There are **two treasure chests** around the southwest corner.
3.  Take the south exit for a short scene.
4.  Head west, pick up the treasure chest near the small tower, and take the southwest exit to **East Trista Highway**.
    *   **East Trista Highway**: Very short area. 3 treasure chests + fishing location to the west. Near the bridge, an event scene plays (Rean notices guards).
5.  Return to West Celdic Highway and take the southeast exit to **Celdic**.

#### Celdic
When you enter Celdic, visit three key event locations (marked with red exclamation marks):
1.  **Becky**: Celdic House (Near West Celdic Highway entrance).
2.  **Margot**: Weathercock Inn (Behind the counter).
3.  **Rosine**: Grand Market.

After conversing with them, an event scene unfolds. Rean purchases **Imperial Chronicle Issue #2**. After reading and exiting, another event triggers.

**Next Steps:**
1.  **Grand Market**: Buy **Gambler Jack Reprint #2** and (if 2nd Playthrough) **Black Records #2** from Cornette's shop (center of market).
2.  **Chapel**: Speak with **Moulbaix** (west end). She teaches the recipe for **Fresh Vegetable Juice**.

**Exit**: Head towards East Celdic Highway.

**Rewards & Items**
*   **Recipe**: Fresh Vegetable Juice (Moulbaix, Celdic Chapel).
*   **Books**:
    *   *Gambler Jack Reprint #2*: Cornette's General Goods (Grand Market).
    *   *Black Records #2*: Cornette's General Goods (Second Playthrough only).
    *   *Gambler Jack II - Chapter 1*: Tarim (Near Farm on East Celdic Highway).

#### East Celdic Highway
1.  **North**: Locate treasure chest containing **Silver Earrings**.
2.  **Northeast Corner**: Talk to **Tarim** (NPC near farmhouse). He gives **Gambler Jack II Chapter 1**.
    *   *Note*: Collect all 14 volumes to exchange for the strongest weapon later. If missed, they can sometimes be found via Inspect Curios (unreliable).
3.  **Southeast Bridge**: Fishing spot. Treasure chest nearby triggers an event (Rean finds an **Old Key**).
4.  **Windmill (Center-South)**: Watch event scene. **Machias** joins your party.

**Return to Celdic**:
*   **Market Manager's House**: Speak to Manager Otto. This adds the Quest section to your Notebook and introduces **AP (Academic Points)**.
    *   *Goal*: Max AP per chapter = **S Rank**.
*   **Chapel**: Talk to **Rosine**. She gives 3 Tear Balms and adds page 1 to her profile.

**Side Quest: Munch no More**
*   **Quest Monster**: East Celdic Highway 2 (Northeast corner).
*   **Action**: Defeat the monster. Choose to return to Celdic and report to Manager Otto.
*   **Reward**: **Detection Quartz** (Displays all treasure chests on mini-map), 1500 Mira, 4 AP.

> **Boss: Giant Crop Muncher**
> *   **HP**: 40,250
> *   **Elemental Efficacy**: Earth (110%), Water (90%), Fire (120%), Wind (100%)
> *   **Minions**: Crop Muncher x7 (HP: 3,680)

**Story Quest: Herbal Remedies**
1.  **Celdic Chapel**: Speak to Father Zirbel behind the altar.
2.  **Grand Market**: Speak to Flint (Northwest stall).
3.  **West Celdic Highway**: Head north near Lunaria Nature Park Entrance. Speak to **Johnson** (NPC near gate).
4.  **Lunaria Nature Park**: Find **Cure Herb** at the northeast corner (near treasure chest).
5.  **Return**: Give herb to Father Zirbel.
*   **Reward**: Teara Balm x2, Celestial Balm x2, 4 AP.

**Advance Story**:
*   Exit Celdic -> East Celdic Highway -> Enter Windmill (South).
*   Examine communication device, select **2nd choice**.
*   Rendezvous at **Point D** (East Celdic Highway 3, Northeast end).
*   **Eliot and Fie** join.
    *   *Note*: Overdrive works between Rean and friends. For others, clear Trial Chests. Burst function and party switching are now available.

#### East Celdic Highway 3
*   **Northeast Corner**: **Sepith Mass x100** (Treasure).
*   **Point D**: Join party members.
*   **Southwest Area**: Pick up all treasure chests.
    *   **Blue Treasure Chest (Trial Chest)**: Requires prearranged party to use Overdrive.
    *   *Tip*: Equip **Faint Resistant** accessory (Citrus Bottle from Lunaria Park helps).
*   **Southwest Exit**: Monster Chest containing **Gladiator Headband**.
*   **Exit**: Southeast to Twin Dragons Bridge.

**Trial Chest Boss: Senior Bear Mole**
*   **HP**: 20,250 (Normal)
*   **Minions**: Bear Mole x4
*   **Item Drop**: Crest R
*   **Skills**: Hammer Swing (Knockback/Faint), Jump Slam (Faint).
*   **Strategy**: Wait for moles to gather, use S-Craft. Machias (Petrify Shell), Eliot (Area Attack/Healer).

#### Twin Dragons Bridge
1.  **Waiting Lounge (North)**: Speak to **Morize** (Northwest corner). She teaches **Cheese Omelet** recipe.
2.  **Event**: Go near the bridge to see a merchant arguing with a soldier.
3.  **Duct**: Examine duct to advance story, go to Garrelia Side.

##### Hidden Quest: A Cry For Help
*   **Start**: Leave Twin Dragons Bridge after the merchant/soldier event. Head north near East Celdic Highway 2.
*   **Location**: East Celdic Highway 3 (near the 100 Sepith Mass chest).
*   **Action**: Defeat monsters. Select **1st choice** to return to Twin Dragons Bridge.
*   **Completion**: Speak to the three merchants (Left, Center, Right) in the Waiting Lounge. Go to the east side of the building for an event.
*   **Reward**: Red Pendulum, 2000 Mira, 4 AP.

#### Twin Dragons Bridge - Garrelia Side
*   **Garrelia Byroad**:
    *   **Merchant (Under Bridge)**: Buy **Contact Lens** (Costume attachment) instead of exchanging U-Material later.
    *   **Treasure**: Celestial Balm (Northwest path), Defense 2 Quartz (North end).
    *   **Trial Chest**: Clear to unlock Overdrive for Eliot and Fie.
*   **Garrelia Byroad 2**:
    *   **Holy Chain**: South path treasure chest.
    *   **EP Charge I**: North path.
    *   **Reaper Quartz**: Southeast end monster chest.
    *   **Garrelia Fortress**: Northeast entrance. **Save Game** at Orbment Station.

**Boss: Xeno & Leonidas**
*   **Type**: Unwinnable battle (Story).
*   **Objective**: Reduce one boss's HP to **30% or below** by turn 40 for Bonus AP.
*   **Strategy**: Full CP party. Buff with Rean, use S-Craft + Overdrive. Use AT Delay skills (Arc Slash, Bullet Cyclone). Elliot as main healer. Watch for S-Crafts (70% damage) and mines (scan for trophy).
*   **AP**: 3 AP for completing the objective.

**Boss: Drakkhen (Sword)**
*   **Mech Battle**: Focus on one unit. Attack **Body** in Idle Stance, **Head** in Offensive Stance.
*   **Drakkhen (Gun)**: Backup Pilot mode active. Attack **Head** in Idle Stance, **Body** in Offensive Stance.
    *   *Rewards*: 100 Link EXP between partners.

**After Battle**:
*   **Resting Area (North)**: Speak to **Arbekker** to receive **Gambler Jack II - Chapter 2**.
*   **Profiles**: Speak to Monica (General Store) and Munk (Dining) to add to notebook.
*   **Exit**: Examine Valimar, select 1st choice.

**Snowboard Mini-Game**: Exit town via Southeast exit.

##### Hidden Quest: Leaf it to Me
*   **Start**: Speak to **Alf & Kiki** (kids near station). Choose **1st option** (Offer assistance).
*   **Location**: **Ymir Valley**.
*   **Task**: Find two colored leaves.
    1.  **Leaf 1**: Northwest corner of Ymir Valley 1.
    2.  **Leaf 2**: Upper Southeast corner of Ymir Valley 2 (near treasure chest).
*   **Completion**: Return to Alf & Kiki.
*   **Note**: Scan the monkey-like monster in Ymir Valley for Battle Trophy.
*   **Reward**: Mishy Plushie, 1500 Mira, 4 AP.

*   **AP Calculation**: The game accumulates all AP earned.
    *   **S-Rank Requirement**: Total of **35 AP**.
