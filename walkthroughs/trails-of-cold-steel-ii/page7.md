# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
## Act 2 Part 2 Walkthrough

### December 19
**Preparation & Initial Steps**
*   Read the quest list on the computer terminal.
*   **Party Selection:** **Millium** is mandatory for this Act.
*   **Becky:** If recruited, she allows item exchange (Pawnshop feature).
*   **Valimar Upgrade:** Sacrifice **1 Attack 2** and **1 Action 2** quartz plus **20 U-Materials** to get the corresponding Ex-Orb for Valimar. This is advisable for the tough Mech battles ahead.
*   **Resource Management:** Spend Mira and Sepith only on the 4 main party members you intend to use for the Act boss. Rotate support characters to gain EXP without upgrading their gear. Swap armor/quartz if the story forces specific characters. Save resources until further notice.

**Activities**
1.  Head to the **Front Deck (3F)**.
    *   Speak to **Celine** (updates profile).
    *   Speak to **Alfin** to receive the **Medal of Charity**.
    *   Play **Blade** with friends to raise Link EXP.
    *   Speak to **Millium** and **Jusis** for a short event.
2.  Speak to **Towa**. The **Celdic** region is added to Courageous Destinations.
    *   **New Cryptids:** Found in *Lunaria Nature Park* and *Nord Highlands - South*. Defeat them now or later.
3.  Visit **Ymir**.
    *   **General Store:** Purchase the **Cheese Fondue Recipe Book**.
    *   **Snow Board Mini-Game:** A new obstacle has been added.
    *   **The Phoenix Wings:** Speak to receptionist **Baggins** to start the side quest.

#### Side Quest: Creeping for Creeps
*   **Client:** Baggins (The Phoenix Wings).
*   **Objective:** In the snowboard mini-game, catch two snow sheep.
*   **Rewards:** **2 Bonus AP**, 500 Sepith Mass, 3,500 Mira, and 3 AP (Total 6 AP if successful).
*   **Note:** You can retry until successful. A mini-boss appears after catching them.
    *   *Mini-Boss Tip:* Use S-Crafts or summon Valimar if the battle is too difficult (freezes party, high single-target damage).

---

### December 20
**Proving Ground & Twin Dragons Bridge**
1.  **Courageous:** Speak to **Towa**, select **Proving Ground**.
2.  **Proving Ground:** Speak to **Munk** (eating table, Southeast) to help find his sticker.
3.  **Courageous:** Equip Valimar with **Attack 1-2** and **Action 1** Ex-Orbs.
4.  **Twin Dragons Bridge:** Select as destination.
    *   Enter the **Command Room** (North door, same as Chapter 2 Act 1 boss).
    *   Speak to **General Craig** to retrieve Munk's Sticker.
    *   Speak to **Captain Gaul** (left) to learn **Seafood Salad** recipe.
    *   Speak to **Fiona** for a scene.
    *   **Examine the bookshelf** to get **Hot Colour** for your Orbal Bike.
5.  **Mech Duel:** Speak to **Major Neithardt** (Southwest, by Panzer Soldat) to accept the quest.

#### Story Quest: I'll Make a Mech Out of You
*   **Objective:** Defeat Major Neithardt in a mech duel.
*   **Rewards:** **2 Extra AP**, Skill Handbook, 3,500 Mira, 3 AP (Total 5 AP), Munk's Profile Page 1.
*   **Strategy:** Neithardt hits hard (15k-17k idle, 34k-37k attack stance). Use **Morning Moon** to evade or keep a healer ready. When HP is low, he uses a sword stance with a 50k-60k attack. Reload and retry if you fail.

**Loot & Exploration**
*   **Twin Dragons Bridge - Central Area:** Find **Flare Bomb** quartz.
*   **Twin Dragons Bridge - Garrelia Side:** Find **Yellow Pendulum** and **Sepith Mass**.
    *   *New Monster:* **Metal Beetle** (available after Dec 18).
*   **Garrelia Byroad 1 (Under Bridge):** Clear the **Trial Chest** to unlock Overdrive for **Laura, Machias, Gaius, and Sara**.
    *   *Boss:* Giga Ashen Pom x2. Weak to area attacks.

#### Story Quest: Just Getting Wormed Up
1.  Go to **Garrelia Byroad 2** (Southeast from Byroad 1).
2.  Defeat the monster in the **southwest corner**.
    *   *Boss:* Abyss Worm x6. Use S-Crafts and Valimar.
3.  **Rewards:** Poison 2, 2,500 Mira, 4 AP.

**Recruitment & New Features**
1.  **Courageous:** Return to speak to **Towa**, then go to **Proving Ground**.
    *   Return sticker to **Munk**. He joins the ship, unlocking **Radio Sweepstakes**.
2.  **Celdic Region:**
    *   **East Celdic Highway (Farm):** Meet **Paula**. Help her find the black horse (requires **Jusis** in party).
        *   Find horse at **Lunaria Nature Park** (Northwest corner, Lunaria Nature Park 2).
        *   **Reward:** Paula joins; unlocks **Horse riding** on field map.
    *   **East Trista Highway:** Find **Alan** and **Loggins** (Southeast corner). Complete scouting request.
        *   **Reward:** They join; unlock **Sword Training Facility**.
    *   **North Kreuzen Highway 3:**
        *   Northeast: Meet **Fidelio**. Accept request.
        *   **Loot:** **Judgement Bolt** (behind Fidelio), **EP Charge III** (West end).
        *   **Trial Chest (Southwest):** For **Jusis** and **Millium**.
            *   *Boss:* Godlinoceros & Enduranoceros.
            *   *Strategy:* Back row, Millium buffs Def/Ultimate Barrier, Jusis uses Fire Arts. Equip Faint prevention for Jusis.
        *   **East Celdic Highway 3:** Find **Dorothee** (Need **Emma** in party). Defeat black sheep to finish Fidelio's request.
3.  **Courageous:**
    *   Speak to **Dorothee** (2F Book Room) to update profile.
    *   Report status at the computer terminal.
    *   Speak to **Towa** (Red option) to end the day.

---

### December 21
**Roer Infiltration**
*   **Party:** **Alisa** and **Rean** are mandatory. You cannot switch later.
*   **Trial Chests:** You can only access **two** of the three available. Recommended:
    1.  **Spina Byroad** (Alisa & Emma)
    2.  **Sachsen Mountain Path** (Eliot & Gaius)

**Spina Byroad**
*   Check fishing spot near boat.
*   Follow linear path to **Trial Chest**.
    *   *Requirement:* **Alisa** and **Emma**.
    *   *Boss:* Ghost Tomartian.
    *   *Strategy:* Equip **Confuse resistance**. Use S-Craft immediately. Fire arts effective.
*   **Loot:** **Celestial Balm**, **Crescent Mirror** (West end).
*   Head east to **Roer**.

**Roer Activities**
*   Visit Key Locations (Red !): **Hugo** (RF Store), **Linde** (General Goods), **Cathedral**, **Instructor Mary** (Residence #1), **Airport**.
*   **Dvance's Diner:** Visit.
*   **General Store:** Buy **Gambler Jack Reprint #11-12**.
*   **Nortia Highway:**
    *   *West Area (Highway 1):* 3 chests (**Tearal Balm**, **Athelas**, Sepith).
    *   *Highway 2:* **Aries Master Quartz** (SE path monster chest), **Nightmare** quartz (North), U-Materials (South).
    *   *Trial Chest (Center path):* Requires **Eliot, Emma, Millium, Sara**.
        *   *Boss:* Pudgy Penguin x2. Weak to Fire and Confuse.
    *   *Highway 3:* **EP Charge III** (Center), **EP Cut 2** (Northwest), **Phantom Phobia** (North, near Ignis Shrine).
    *   **Schwarz Drache Barrier:** Go SW exit (Rewards **2 AP**). Speak to **Margarita**.
*   **Sachsen Mountain Path:**
    *   *North/Northeast:* **Suzaku** Quartz (Monster Chest).
    *   *West:* **Celestial Balm**.
    *   *South:* **Red Pendulum**.
    *   *Path 2 (Northwest Exit):* **Dragon Skin Boots** (West), **Cross Crusade** quartz (SW), **Tearal Balm** (NE).
    *   **Trial Chest (Northwest):** Requires **Eliot** and **Gaius**.
        *   *Boss:* Master Cyron.
        *   *Strategy:* Equip **Freeze resistance**. Gaius uses S-Craft on mobs, Hunter Wing on boss. Eliot heals.
*   **Iron Mines:** Fight Jaegers -> West Exit (Jaegers) -> Train (Fight rooms) -> Northwest Exit to **RF Building**.

**RF Building**
*   **5F:** **Celestial Balm** (SE room), **Sepith Mass** (West room), **Dragon Skin Jumpsuit** (SE corner).
*   **12F (Elevator):** **Grim Butterfly** quartz (North), **EP Charge III** (Left room), **Curia Balm** (Center path, right room), **Dark Pendulum** (SE room).
*   **20F (Elevator):** **Sepith** (North), **Tearal Balm** (SW path), **Black Bell** quartz (SE room).
    *   **Unlock Room:** Examine computer in SW room to unlock NW room.
    *   **Katze Master Quartz:** Found in the newly unlocked NW room.
*   **23F (Elevator):** Heal, Save, then Boss.

#### Boss: Regenenkopf Type-0
*   **Preparation:** **Rean** with Canceller Set. Equip **Confuse resistance** (Alisa: True Gladiator Belt). Link Rean & Alisa.
*   **Strategy:**
    1.  Defeat **Shigure** adds first (they heal/buff boss).
    2.  Boss is weak to status down. Use AT Delay (Rean's Arc Slash + Alisa's Heaven's Gift).
    3.  At 25% HP, boss heals and gains Insight. Use S-Crafts to burst it down.

**Post-Boss**
*   Save and change Ex-Orbs.
*   **Critical:** Equip **Attack 1-2** and **Action 1** Ex-Orbs only. (Important for N3 Run).

**Bonding & Final Activities**
