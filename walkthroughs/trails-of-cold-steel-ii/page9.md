### Nightmare Tips & Preparation

**A: Two-Team Battle Prep**
At the end of this Act, you will fight two consecutive battles with different teams (including guest characters).
*   **Weapons:** Craft 10 weapons for Class VII (Sara does not participate).
*   **Quartz:** Assemble Attack Stacks (Attack 3) for high STR members (Rean, Laura, Gaius, Millium).
*   **Accessories:** Obtain 4 Warrior/Gladiator Badge accessories. Build a **Canceler Set** (Rean) and **Dodge Set** (Fie).
*   **ARCUS:** Ensure Rean, Laura, Fie, and Emma have two Level 2 slots (one elemental, one non-elemental).

**B: Sepith & Level Building**
*   **Goal:** 90,000+ Mira and ample Fire/Time Sepith.
*   **Method:** Convert excess Water/Earth Sepith (batches of 1000) to Mira.
*   **Grinding:** Fight every enemy in the 3 remaining Shrines. Complete Trial Chests (2 per cave) for grinding.
*   **Target Level:** Aim for **Lv 107–109** for fighters/tanks.

**C: Alisa & Beryl**
*   **Alisa:** If active in boss fights, she permanently gains the **True Gladiator Belt** and links to Rean (switching to Fie if needed).
*   **Snowboarding:** Complete the new course to get the **Passionate Rouge** accessory.
*   **Ymir Valley Event:** Manually enter Ymir Valley (Quick Travel won't trigger it). Go to the very end to find **Beryl**. Speak to her to recruit her.
*   **Fortune Teller:** Return to Courageous and speak to Beryl in the **Fortune Room (2F, SE room)** to check relationship status and bonding trophy eligibility.

### Legram: Side Quests & Exploration

**Travel to Legram.**
1.  **Arseid Mansion (NW):** Speak to **Josef** to learn the **Mixed Grill** recipe.
2.  **Bracer Guild:** Speak to **Klaus** or **Miles** to start side quests.

#### Side Quest: Mist Opportunity
*   **Client:** Klaus (Bracer Guild).
*   **Requirement:** **Emma** must be in your party.
*   **Location:** Lohengrin Castle.
*   **Treasures:**
    *   **Chakravarti Gem:** Lohengrin Castle - Entrance Hall (East side).
    *   **EP Charge IV:** Opposite side of the gem.
    *   **Dark Emblem:** Left Wing.
*   **Boss: Lucifuge** (HP: 156,170)
    *   **Mechanic:** Summons Shadow Spirits. Defeat spirits to force boss to summon more instead of attacking.
    *   **Soul Pinon:** Incapacitates a party member and drains HP. Use **S-Craft** to release them instantly.
    *   **Strategy:** Use S-Crafts to clear spirits quickly.
*   **Reward:** Youkai Gem, 4,000 Mira, **4 AP**.
    *   *Note (2nd Playthrough):* Find **Black Records #5** in the bottom left corner.

### Bareahard Section

**Travel to Bareahard.**
1.  **Fortnum Books (Christie's Galleria 2F):** Purchase **Gambler Jack Reprints**.
2.  **Noble District (House #2):** Speak to **Bridget** and call out **Alan** to recruit **Bridget**.
3.  **Sorciere Restaurant (Upstairs):** Speak to **Caroline** (Accept request).
    *   **Requirement:** **Alisa** must be in party.
    *   **Location:** Station Street -> Entrance of Underground Waterway.
    *   **Event:** Speak to **Theresia**, bring **Emily** to recruit her.
    *   **Reward:** Access to Shooting Range Lv 4-5 (Clear all 5 levels for **Cypher** Master Quartz).
4.  **Turner's Jewelers (Artisans' Street):** Speak to **Colette**.
    *   **Task:** Find 4 **Dryad's Tear** on **South Kreuzen Highway** (marked with green exclamation).
    *   **Reward:** Recruit **Colette**.
5.  **North Kreuzen Highway 2:** Now accessible. Contains a new fishing spot and a **Trial Chest** (Alisa/Elliot/Laura/Jusis).

**Trial Chest: North Kreuzen Highway 2**
*   **Bosses:** King Falena x2, Were Falena x4.
*   **Preparation:** Equip **Sleep resistant accessory** on Elliot (mobs use gas attacks).
*   **Strategy:** Use S-Crafts or Alisa's **Heavenly Gift** (Insight/CP Recovery).

**Travel to Aurochs Canyon -> Aurochs Fort.**
*   **Fishing:** Spot near entrance.
*   **Treasures:** Sepith, Celestial Balm EX, Qilin, Impede 2, Shadow Blade.
*   **Trial Chest (Canyon 2):** For Machias/Jusis.
*   **Side Quest Monster (Canyon 3):** See below.

#### Side Quest: Canyon, or CAN'Tyon?
*   **Location:** Southeast corner of Aurochs Canyon 3.
*   **Boss: Volkeron** (HP: 146,640)
    *   **Preparation:**
        *   **Formation:** Bait with Fie on top.
        *   **Quartz:** Freeze/Freeze II or Sleep on all. Emma with strongest Water art.
        *   **Accessories:** Burn protection. Fie with Task equipment (Agl boost).
        *   **Links:** Alisa and Fie.
    *   **Strategy:** Delay boss with Fie's **Bullet Cyclone** or Rean's **Arc Slash**. Use Water Arts for damage. Freeze/Sleep is highly effective.
*   **Reward:** **Rakshasha** quartz, 3,000 Mira, **4 AP**.

**Aurochs Fort:**
*   Speak to **Rex**, bring **Fidelio** to recruit him.
*   **Rest Area - Crescent Moon:** Speak to **Spare** to get **Gambler Jack II Chapter 13**.

### Shrine Trials Part B

Clear the remaining three shrines.
1.  **Shrine 2 Boss: Magic Knight Heavy Ruby** (HP: 199,127)
    *   **Strategy:** Buff early. Use **Arc Slash** (Rean) and **Radiant Wings** (Laura). Main damage from **Earth Cleaver** (Laura). Spam S-Crafts when HP drops to 60-70k.
2.  **Shrine 3 Boss: Magic Knight Isla-Zeriel** (HP: 210,210)
    *   **Strategy:** Identical to Heavy Ruby, slightly higher stats.
3.  **Shrine 4 Boss: Grianos-Aura** (HP: 249,843)
    *   **Preparation:** Emma with **Might Set** and Elemental Bell quartz. Rean with **Zeus Gem**. Freeze/Confuse resistant accessories.
    *   **Strategy:**
        *   Overdrive Rean/Emma immediately.
        *   Rean: Spd buffs.
        *   Emma: **Crescent Shell** (re-apply every turn, especially after boss attacks). Use arts/S-Crafts.
        *   Boss attacks can freeze/confuse; ensure resistance.

**Post-Shrine Prep:**
*   Collect all **Zemurian Crystals**.
*   Craft **Attack Ex-Orbs** for Valimar.
*   **Backup Save** at the Courageous.
*   Defeat remaining **Cryptids** if missed.
*   Speak to **George** at the Hangar (Red option) to advance story.
    *   *Note:* Party auto-boosted to Lv 108 (Sara to Lv 111).

#### The Final Operation
Speak to **Towa** at the bridge.
*   **Team A:** Mandatory **Towa**. (Recommended: Alisa, Jusis/Emma).
*   **Team B:** Mandatory **Angelica**. (Recommended: Laura, Fie, Machias).
*   **Divine Knight Battle:** Select your **Link Partner**. This choice affects the final bonding event trophy.

**Mech Battle (Goliath B & Kestrel B):**
*   **Strategy:** Target **Goliath B** first to build CP. Heal under 50% HP. Use **Morning Moon** at 200 CP.
*   **Note:** Kestrel B is fastest and will counter-attack often.

**Team B Boss Battle: Thors Academy**
*   **Enemies:** Vincent, Sariffa, Edel, Friedel.
*   **Strategy:**
    *   **Laura:** Brilliant Spin (gather) -> Brilliant Ember (Mute Sariffa).
    *   **Fie:** Bullet Cyclone (Status/AT Delay).
    *   **Angelica:** Focus **Sariffa** first (she heals). Use Zero Impact.
    *   **Machias:** Burst Drive.
    *   **Tip:** Use status ailments (Seal). S-Craft when below 50%.

**Team A Boss Battle: Thors Academy**
*   **Enemies:** Patrick, Celestin, Ferris, Lambert.
*   **Strategy:**
    *   **Towa:** A-Detector (Scan enemies).
    *   **Rean:** Chrono Burst, then Arc Slash.
    *   **Alisa:** Heavenly Gift.
    *   **Focus:** **Celestin** first (heals party). Cast arts before Ferris (she can Impede).
    *   **Tip:** Use S-Crafts to finish.
