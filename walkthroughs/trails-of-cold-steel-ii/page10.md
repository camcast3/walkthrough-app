## December 30 Night Time

Once you have regained control, there is a ton of side activities you need to do first.

### Key Activities & Rewards
*   **Tomato Pot Recipe:** Go to Trista, near the entrance of the **Upper Class Dorm**, speak to **Lotte**.
*   **Gambler Jack II - Finale:** Go back to Thors and enter the **school library**. Speak to Librarian **Karol**.
*   **Zemurian Ore:** If you have collected all chapters of Gambler Jack II, head to **Kirsche's Cafe & Inn** and speak to **Vice Principal Heinrich**. He will give you the ore (allows forging the strongest weapon) and update his profile.
*   **Fishing Spots:** Two spots in Trista:
    1.  Pond at the north end of the school.
    2.  River at the northeast of town.
*   **Fiery Colour (Bike):** Head to the **house located northeast of Trista**. Examine the bookshelf to the right of the staircase on the bottom floor.
*   **Character Profiles:** Speak to all NPCs listed below to update their profiles.
*   **Medal of Love:** Speak to **Alfin** at the principal office (after speaking to all NPCs).
*   **Event:** Take the **west exit of Trista**.
*   **Towa:** Speak to **Towa** at the school rooftop.

### Chosen Partner Event
**Conditions to see the yellow '!' on the map:**
*   **Towa and Alfin:**
    *   Character's Link Level is 6 or higher.
    *   Have seen their Bonding Event in Bareahard at the end of Act 3.
    *   Character has seen one of the 2 Key Bonding Events.
    *   Link Level must be 5 or higher.

**How to Choose:**
1.  Finish all side activities.
2.  Speak to the character and choose the **1st choice**.
3.  You can still change your mind by talking to another character and choosing the 1st option.
4.  Go back to **Class VII's dorm** and choose to enter.
5.  **Rewards:** You get the corresponding Trophy. If your partner reaches Link Level 7, you get the **Link Master Trophy**.

## December 31st

*   **Final S-Craft:** Rean learns his final S-Craft.
*   **Crafting:** Head to the 1st floor and speak to **George**. Use the Zemurian Ore to craft the strongest weapon.
*   **Imperial Chronicle Issue #6:** Available at any book store.
*   **Absentee Father Quest:** Speak to Zirbel at Trista's Chapel.

### Cooking & Rewards
*   **Superb Dishes:** Give all 22 superb dishes to **Monica** (2nd floor Cafeteria) for the **Impenetrable Shield** quartz.
*   **Peculiar Dishes:** Give all peculiar dishes to **Margarita** (northern end of the corridor) for the **Chaos** quartz.
*   **All 22 Recipes:** Speak to **Ferris** in the Game Room for the **Genesis** master quartz.
*   **Unique Dishes:** Give every type of Unique dish to **Bridget** (3rd floor) for the **Revolution** quartz.

### Black Records (2nd Playthrough)
*   Speak to **Instructor Thomas** (Book Room, 2F) with all Black Records for a trophy.
*   **Missing a Record?** Check **Micht** in Trista or **Becky** (3rd floor of Courageous). Leaving and re-entering Micht's store refreshes his stock.

### Optional Dungeon: Glacia Shrine
*   **Unlock:** Head to Ymir and speak to Rean's father at the **Baron's Mansion**.
*   **Location:** Very west end of **Ymir Valley 1**.
*   **Reward:** **Ice Crown** accessory (10 CP Regeneration/turn, +50% Critical Up) and **The Frost Hunter** trophy.

### Side Quests (Zemurian Ore Shards)
Each completed quest rewards a **Zemurian Ore Shard**.

#### Side Quest: My Jormungand Don't Want None
*   **Location:** End of East Celdic Highway 3 (Northeast end).
*   **Boss:** Jormungand (172,254 HP).
*   **Weakness:** Faint effects work well. Canceler build recommended.
*   **Reward:** Zemurian Ore Shard, 3500 Mira, 4 AP.

#### Side Quest: Sachsen Violence
*   **Location:** Sachsen Iron Mines -> Sachsen Mountain Path 2 (Northeast near fishing spot).
*   **Boss:** Gespard (178,653 HP).
*   **Weakness:** High physical defense; use Arts. Impede is effective.
*   **Reward:** Zemurian Ore Shard, 3500 Mira, 4 AP.

#### Side Quest: Nord by Nord-east
*   **Location:** Nord Highlands - Northeast (Center area).
*   **Boss:** Glider Quaker (139,380 HP).
*   **Weakness:** Weak to Mute (silences Thunder Wave). Low HP, S-Crafts work well.
*   **Reward:** Zemurian Ore Shard, 3500 Mira, 4 AP.

#### Side Quest: Ymir, Far, Wherever You Are
*   **Location:** Ymir Valley - End (Left side).
*   **Boss:** Sasquatch (183,654 HP).
*   **Weakness:** Confuse status effective. Canceler strategy works.
*   **Reward:** Zemurian Ore Shard, 3500 Mira, 4 AP.

### Hidden Quest: Absentee Father
*   **Start:** Speak to Father Paulo at Trista's Chapel.
*   **Process:** Speak to head priests in major towns in this order (bring Sara/Gaius for scene changes):
    1.  Celdic Chapel - Zirbel
    2.  Bareahard Cathedral - Amon
    3.  Legram Chapel - Hamilcar
    4.  Roer Cathedral - Homs
    5.  Ymir Chapel - Bernard
*   **Report:** Use Courageous computer.
*   **Reward:** **Lion Heart** accessory, Gold Trophy (315 AP), Acala Gem, 4500 Mira, 4 AP.

### Additional Activities
*   **East Trista Highway:** Explore for new monsters (Battle Notes) and treasure.
    *   **Altair Cannon:** East Trista Highway 2.
    *   **Hades Gem:** East Trista Highway 1.
*   **Battle Notes:** Speak to **Stefan** (Computer Room, 2F) if you have 284 notes for the **Calamity** master quartz. (Missing Metal Beetle? Check Twin Dragon Bridge - Garrelia Side after Dec 18).
*   **Optional Scenes:** Visit Scarlet (Albarea Mansion), Proving Ground resting area, Professor Schmitt (Kirsche).

### Final Mission Preparations
*   **Cook:** 15+ Tomato Hotpots (Seasonal Hotpot) and 20-30 Speed-boosting foods.
*   **Commence:** Start the operation with Towa.

## Final Mission

Select your party (Machias is mandatory).

### Karel Imperial Villa
1.  **Entrance:** Fight Alliance Forces.
2.  **Villa Interior:** Defeat soldiers, heal at Orbment Station.
3.  **Treasures:**
    *   **North Chest:** Claiomh Solarion.
    *   **South Chest:** Seraphic Ring.
    *   **Outside (North Exit):** U-Materials, Tear All Balm, **Goldia Key** (monster chest).
4.  **Villa 3:** Enter south, use Goldia Key, fight soldiers.
    *   **Strategy:** Use Laura (Warrior Set + Zemurian Ore Weapon) against high Def enemies. Use Emma/Elliot with Aries MQ + Shadow Blade.
5.  **Boss:** Altina & G-Zephyranthes.
    *   **Strategy:** Kill G-Zephyranthes first (high Def, use Arts/S-Craft). Altina reflects physical attacks (Black Barrier) and uses Brionac (Laser). Use Crescent Mirror/Shells to reflect.
    *   **Reward:** **Grand Cordon** accessory (+100 STR/DEF/ATS/ADF, +10% Crit) if you have all Medals from Alfin.


### Final Boss: Divine Knight "Ordine" (Mech Battle)

**Stats (Normal Mode)**
| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 350,000 |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | Earth: 100, Water: 110, Fire: 100, Wind: 100 |

**Attack Patterns & Weaknesses**
| Stance | Quote | Weakness (Body/Head/Arm) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Idle** | - | B: ★★☆☆, H: ★☆☆☆, A: ★★★☆ |
| **Attack 1** | "Arghh!" | B: ★★★☆, H: ★★☆☆, A: ★☆☆☆ |
| **Attack 2** | "Come and get me!" | B: ★☆☆☆, H: ★★★☆, A: ★★☆☆ |
| **Attack 3** | "Prepare yourself!" | B: ☆☆☆☆, H: ☆☆☆☆, A: ★★★★ |
| **Attack 4** | "Prepare yourself!" | B: ☆☆☆☆, H: ★★★★, A: ☆☆☆☆ |

**Boss Skills**
*   **Normal Attack:** Weakness: **Arm**
*   **Charge 1:** Weakness: **Body** (*Arc Slash*)
*   **Charge 2:** Weakness: **Head*** (*Criminal Edge*)
*   **Charge 3:** Weakness: **Arm** (*Chaos Saber*)
*   **Charge 4:** Weakness: **Head*** (*Blade Throw*)
    *\*Note: Charge 2 and Charge 4 share the same quote.*

**Strategy**
This boss is challenging due to his speed and ability to unbalance and counter.
*   **Pattern:** Starts with 1 → 2 → 3 → 1. After taking 50-60% HP, the pattern becomes 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 1.
*   **Countering:** Build CP for **Morning Moon** to counter his attacks based on his unbalance weakness.
    *   If **Head** is the likely weakness, use Morning Moon or Guard.
    *   If **Charge 3** (Weak: Arm) is coming, use Morning Moon to deal 2 hits and dodge his deadliest attacks.
*   **Follow-Ups:** If he unbalances you 4 times in a row, he will use a Follow-Up. If he does nothing on the 4th unbalance, a stronger Follow-Up is coming after 5 consecutive unbalances (starts appearing in the second half of the fight).
*   **Support:**
    *   **Elliot:** Healing.
    *   **Alisa:** CP Regeneration.
    *   **Sara:** Strength Down on the boss.

### Final Boss: Vermillion Apocalypse (Phase 1)

**Stats (Normal Mode)**
| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 250,000 |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | All Elements: 100 |
| **Weaknesses** | Slash: ★☆☆☆, Thrust: ★☆☆☆, Pierce: ★☆☆☆, Strike: ★☆☆☆ |

**Preparation**
*   **Formation:** Cannot choose initially; regroup quickly into **Baiting** or **Tank 2**.
*   **Quartz:**
    *   AoE Arts: `Shadow Blade`, `Altair Cannon`, `Claiomh Solarion`.
    *   **Chrono Burst** on someone other than Rean.
    *   **Seraphic Ring** on 2 characters (Main healing source).
    *   One weak art (for dispelling barriers).
    *   **Elliot** with **Aries MQ** (requires Speed bonus).
*   **Accessories:** Cover status ailments (Faint, Death, Confusion). Give **Gladiator Headbands** to Sara and Gaius.
*   **Links:** Swap constantly to use Rush/Overdrive.
*   **Party:** Elliot, Fie, Gaius, Machias, Sara.

**Strategies**
*   **Baiting (Safest):**
    *   **Fie:** Immediately attacks the boss to draw aggro while the party buffs.
    *   **Sara/Gaius:** Use `Thunder Roar` and `Gale Storm`. Avoid `Flash EX` and `Savage Fang II` to maintain formation.
    *   **Elliot/Machias:** Healing, buffing, and using `Burst Drive` (Machias) to disrupt turn order.
    *   **Impassion:** Use on Fie to maximize her CP usage speed.
*   **S-Craft Spam:**
    *   **Fie:** Attack with `Bullet Cyclone II` every turn.
    *   **Gaius:** Buff, use `Twin Cyclones` (S-Craft), then `Wild Rage` once, feed `Seasonal Hotpot`.
    *   **Support:** Back row alternates between supporting Fie and Gaius.
*   **Cancelling:**
    *   Regroup the **whole party** next to the boss.
    *   Use `Resonance Beat` and spam `Chrono Drive`/`Chrono Break` with Elliot to debuff Speed.
    *   Attacks like `Bullet Cyclone II` have a chance to delay the boss.
    *   If Fie/Sara successfully delay, Gaius can use an S-Craft.
    *   **Warning:** Dispel any Reflect barriers with weak crafts/arts. Keep HP topped with `Holy Song` or `Seraphic Ring`. Use `Burst Drive` (Machias) to ensure Elliot gets turns.
    *   **Finish:** When HP reaches 60,000, spam S-Crafts.

### Final Boss: Vermillion Apocalypse (Phase 2)

**Stats (Normal Mode)**
| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 393,960 (Main) + 36,790 (Vermillion Weapon x2) |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | All Elements: 100 |
| **Weaknesses** | Slash: ★★☆☆, Thrust: ★★☆☆, Pierce: ★★☆☆, Strike: ★★☆☆ |

**Preparation**
*   **Formation/Quartz/Accessories:** Same as Phase 1.
*   **Party:** Use the reserve team (members not used in Phase 1).

**Strategies**
*   **S-Craft (Recommended):**
    *   **Tank:** Use `Platinum Shield` or `Adamantine Shield` to mitigate the 15,000+ HP all-hitting attack.
    *   **Buff:** Speed boost ASAP.
    *   **Combo:** Use `Spirit Unification` + `Radiant Lion` repeatedly. Recharge CP with `Seasonal Hotpot`.
    *   **Goal:** 6-7 combos to reach the HP Mark.
*   **Cancelling:**
    *   **Condition:** Only attempt if Rean is Lv 130 and fully decked to stop the boss from acting.
    *   **Alternative:** Use Rean's extra turns to apply Shields, Mirrors, and debuffs.
    *   **Rush:** If not using the full cancel, rush the boss with Rean and Laura at 3 Bravery Points. Abusing `Radiant Lion` is key.
    *   **Caution:** This phase hits harder and summons mobs. Ensure Alisa has `Insight`.

### Final Boss: Vermillion Apocalypse (Phase 3 - Mech Battle)

**Stats (Normal Mode)**
| Stat | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **HP** | 430,000 |
| **Elemental Efficacy** | Earth: 100, Water: 110, Fire: 100, Wind: 100 |

**Attack Patterns & Weaknesses**
| Stance | Weakness (Body/Head/Arm) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Idle** | B: ★☆☆☆, H: ★★☆☆, A: ★★★★ |
| **Attack (Kneeling)** | B: ★★★☆, H: ★★☆☆, A: ★☆☆☆ |
| **Attack (Bending)** | B: ★★☆☆, H: ★★★★, A: ★☆☆☆ |

**Strategy**
*   **Mech Battle:** You team up with **Ordine**.
*   **Constraints:** No support items/arts. Defense is the only mitigation.
*   **Key Rule:** **Keep Ordine alive.** If he falls, you lose crucial damage.
*   **Pattern:** Normal (Arm) → Charge 1 (Body) → Charge 2 (Head) → Repeat.
*   **Execution:**
    *   Use offensive crafts to build Bravery Points quickly.
    *   Unleash **Burst** attacks.
    *   **Caution:** At 50% HP, the boss can Rush/Burst your party if comboed. This can kill Ordine due to his lower defense.
    *   Establish a rhythm and attack the correct weak points to finish quickly.
