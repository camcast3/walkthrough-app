This boss isn't particularly challenging, but it does have a substantial amount of HP. With a full party, Alisa can use her craft, "Heavenly Gift," which will automatically regenerate your CP and provide the "Insight" buff. This allows you to easily dodge some of the boss's attacks. The boss is weak against fire-based attacks, so Fire arts, such as "Heatwave," equipped on Alisa, work effectively. Using all of your party's S-Crafts can quickly take down the boss without much trouble. Be cautious of the boss's strongest attack, "Frost Edge," which it can cast rapidly, potentially causing the Freeze ailment on your party. In the worst-case scenario, you can equip freeze freeze-resistant accessory before the fight starts.
