# The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel II
## Act 1 - Part 2 Walkthrough

### December 5th

#### Overview
*   **Start:** Leave town through **Ymir Valley**.
*   **Party Selection:** Choose **two** members from Class VII and **one** guest (Claire or Toval). This choice is permanent for this segment.
*   **Key Tip:** If you bring Toval or Claire, their S-Crafts can be very useful for early boss fights.

---

#### Nord Highlands - South (Part A)
After the initial scene, you warp to the northwest. Use the map to track treasure chests.

*   **Zender Gate:**
    *   Trigger the event to receive a **Horse**.
    *   Speak to **Marx** in the Mess Hall to learn the **Potato Croquette** recipe.
    *   **Shop:** Buy new weapons here (skip armor; better options await at Lake Lacrima).
    *   **NPCs:** Find **Beryl** (Mess Hall) and **Mint** (outside).
    *   *Note:* The southeast area is locked. Return later for chests.
*   **Nord Settlement (Northeast):**
    *   Head here to trigger a boss battle.

**Boss: Jaegers (Greatsword & Rifle)**
| Boss | HP | Weakness | Drops | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- :--- |
| Jaeger - Greatsword | 11,229 | None (Resist all) | Curia Balm, Reviving Balm | High damage (2k-3.5k/hit). They can resurrect allies. Use S-Crafts immediately. |
| Jaeger - Rifle | 10,488 | None (Resist all) | Teara Balm, EP Charge II | Focus fire to prevent resurrection chains. |

> **Tip:** Buff your party and unleash S-Crafts from Toval or Claire to cheese this fight.

---

#### Lake Lacrima
*   **Gaius** joins your party.
*   **Elder's House:** Speak to **Old Lady Jedah** for the **Hot Milk Tea** recipe.
*   **Doctor's House:** Speak to **San** (next to Dr. Amr) for **Gambler Jack II - Chapter 3**.
*   **General Store:** Buy **Gambler Jack Reprint #3**.
*   **Activity:** Fishing is available at the pier.

---

#### Nord Highlands - North & Northeast
*   **Route:** Head to **Nord Highlands - Northeast** via the southeast exit (Gaius blocks the south path).
*   **Treasure:**
    *   *South Corner:* **Floral Bottle**.
    *   *North Area:* **Harvest** quartz.
*   **Boss: Unsurtr**
    *   **HP:** 48,360
    *   **Weakness:** **Fire** (150% efficacy).
    *   **Danger:** *Frost Edge* and *Icicle Missiles* can inflict **Freeze**.
    *   **Strategy:**
        *   Equip **Freeze-resistant** accessories.
        *   Alisa's *Heavenly Gift* provides CP regen and **Insight** (dodge buff).
        *   Use Fire arts (e.g., Alisa's *Heatwave*) and S-Crafts.

**Post-Boss:** You return to Lake Lacrima. Fast travel is now unlocked.
*   **Trial Chest (Nord Highlands - North Center):** Unlocks **Overdrive** for Alisa and Millium.
*   **Side Quests:** Speak to the Elder for quest logs.

---

### Side Quests & Dungeons (December 5th)

#### 1. Side Quest: Highlands Hunt
*   **Client:** Lacan (Elder's House).
*   **Task:** Defeat 3 groups of **Galion Cougar** in Nord Highlands - North.
*   **Reward:** Nord Bracelet, All Monster Ingredients x3, 1,500 Mira, **4 AP**.

#### 2. Side Quest: Foaling Around
*   **Client:** Sheeda (Northwest Lake Lacrima).
*   **Task:** Find the foal in 3 locations:
    1.  Southeast corner of Nord Highlands - Northeast.
    2.  Northeast end of Nord Highlands - Northeast.
    3.  Nord Settlement.
*   **Reward:** Food Ingredients x3, 2,000 Mira, **4 AP**.

#### 3. Dungeon: Aria Shrine (Nord Highlands - Northeast)
*   **Reward for Clearing:** **3 AP**.
*   **Key Items:**
    *   *Trial Chest:* For Alisa & Gaius.
    *   *Chests:* **Aerial** quartz, **Sleep** quartz, **EP Charge II**, **Sepith Mass**, **Criminal** (Master Quartz).
*   **Boss: Tildawn - Pazuu x3**
    *   **Weakness:** **Time, Space, Mirage**.
    *   **Danger:** *Black Energy* inflicts **Sleep**; *Aerial* (Wind art) deals massive damage.
    *   **Strategy:**
        *   Equip **Sleep-resistant** accessories.
        *   Use **Impede** arts to cancel enemy casting.
        *   Gaius can tank with *Insight*; use Alisa to heal/regen CP.

#### 4. Hidden Quest: Zats Amore
*   **Client:** Zats or Manning (Zender Gates, Store Area).
*   **Task:** Examine 4 tank wrecks in Nord Highlands - South (marked with green exclamation points). Find a pendant in the 4th wreck and return it to Zats.
*   **Reward:** EP Charge x5, 2,000 Mira, **4 AP**.

---

#### Nord Highlands - South (Part B) & Watchtower
*   **Missable:**
    *   Speak to **Officer Ogram** (Zender Gates) for **Gambler Jack II - Chapter 4**.
    *   Open the **Trial Chest** (Southeast area) to complete Overdrive for other characters.
*   **Watchtower:**
    *   Enter via the southeast corner of Nord Highlands - South (red exclamation mark).
    *   **Enemies:** Alliance Soldiers (weak to **Seal**), Mercenaries.
    *   **Key Items:**
        *   *1F:* **Wind Shoes**
        *   *1F Duct:* **La Forte** quartz
        *   *2F:* Sepith x100
        *   *3F Duct:* **Attack 2** quartz
        *   *4F:* **EP Charge II**
    *   **Boss: Bleublanc & Atina**
        *   **Condition:** Defeat within **40 turns** for bonus AP (3 AP).
        *   **Strategy:**
            *   Enter with full CP. Unleash all S-Crafts immediately.
            *   Use **Alisa's Heavenly Gift** for CP regen mid-fight.
            *   Atina uses *Black Barrier* (blocks 1 physical hit); attack her to nullify it.
            *   Defeat summoned adds immediately.
    *   **Final Boss: Mech Battle (Drakkhen & Spiegel)**
        *   Focus on one unit at a time.
        *   Use **Fie's** speed buff for extra turns.
        *   Use co-pilot **Shinki** to heal or **Charge** for CP if EP runs low.
        *   Target the **body** of the Spiegel unit regardless of stance.

### December 8

#### Ymir Town Activities
*   **General Store (Camilla):**
    *   Start Hidden Quest: **Azuki's Adventure**.
    *   Buy **Onion Soup** recipe.
    *   Buy **Imperial Chronicle Issue #3**.
*   **Residence (Southeast of Tavern):** Speak to **Masha** for **Gambler Jack II - Chapter 5**.
*   **Snowboard Mini-Game:** Complete the new difficulty for the **Hare** quartz.
*   **Character Profiles:**
    *   Speak to **Claire** (Southeast of Baron's Mansion) → Claire Page 2.
    *   Attend **Sharon's** bonding event (Baron's Mansion Kitchen) → Sharon Page 1.
    *   Attend **Toval's** bonding event (Near Station) → Toval Page 2.
*   **Ymir Valley:** New monster **Snow Rat** appears (scan for trophy).

#### Hidden Quest: Azuki's Adventure
*   **Client:** Camilla (General Store).
*   **Task:** Talk to these 4 NPCs:
    1.  **Werner** (Phoenix Wings Kitchen)
    2.  **Gerald** (Tavern, behind counter)
    3.  **Luke** (Near Station)
    4.  **Sister Lisa** (Septian Church)
*   **Reward:** Ymir Manjuu x10, 2,000 Mira, **4 AP**.
